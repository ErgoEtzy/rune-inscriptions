## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Una/"Unna let ræisa þennsa stæin æftiR sun sinn "Øystæin/"Æistæin, sum do i hvitavaðum. "Guð hialpi salu hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Una/"Unna lét reisa þenna stein eptir son sinn "Eystein, sem dó í hvítaváðum. "Guð hjalpi sálu hans.
```

## Translation to English

Una/Unna had this stone raised in memory of her son Eysteinn who died in christening robes. May God help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Una/Unna lät resa denna sten efter sin son Östen, som dog i dopkläder. Gud hjälpe hans själ.
