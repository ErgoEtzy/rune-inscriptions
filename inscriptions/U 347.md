## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Hlífsteinn lét gera sér til sálubótar ok sinni konu "Ingirún ok sínum sonum "Jôrundr ok "Nikulás ok "Loðinn brúar.
```

## Translation to English

Hlífsteinn had the bridges made for himself for the salvation of his soul and that of his wife Ingirún and of their sons Jôrundr and Nikulás and Loðinn.

## Translation to Swedish

Livsten lät göra broarna till själabot för sig och sin hustru Ingerun och sina söner Jorund och Niklas och Luden.
