## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Sighvatr ok "Þorbiorn ok "ÞorgrimR ok "Ærinmundr letu ræisa stæin æftiR broður sinn "Sigstæin. Hann do i "Viborgum.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sighvatr ok "Þorbjôrn ok "Þorgrímr ok "Erinmundr létu reisa stein eptir bróður sinn "Sigstein. Hann dó í "Véborgum.
```

## Translation to English

Sighvatr and Þorbjôrn and Þorgrímr and Erinmundr had the stone raised in memory of their brother Sigsteinn. He died in Véborg.

## Translation to Swedish

Sigvat och Torbjörn och Torgrim och Ärenmund lät resa stenen efter sin broder Sigsten. Han dog i Viborg.
