## Transliteration

```text
þuri ÷ auk frystain ' litu raisa stain iftiR ' þurstain broþur sin
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"ÞoriR ok "Frøystæinn letu ræisa stæin æftiR "Þorstæin, broður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þórir ok "Freysteinn létu reisa stein eptir "Þorstein, bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þórir and Freysteinn had the stone raised in memory of Þorsteinn, their brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Tore och Frösten lät resa stenen efter Torsten, sin broder.
