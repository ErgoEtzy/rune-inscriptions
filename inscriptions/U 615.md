## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Sigviðr let ræisa stæin þannsa aft "Brusa, faður sinn. "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sigviðr lét reisa stein þenna ept "Brúsa, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Sigviðr had this stone raised in memory of Brúsi, his father. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Sigvid lät resa denna sten efter Bruse, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
