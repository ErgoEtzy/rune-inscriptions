
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ófeigr ok "Krókr ok "Steinn ok "Helgi létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Svein, fôður sinn, ok at "Botna, bróður sinn, ok "Inga. "Guð hjalpi ônd þeira.
```

## Translation to English

Ófeigr and Krókr and Steinn and Helgi had this stone raised in memory of Sveinn, their father, and in memory of Botni, their brother, and Inga (also raised). May God help their spirits.

## Translation to Swedish

Ofeg och Krok och Sten och Helge och Inga lät resa denna sten efter Sven, sin fader, och efter Botne, sin broder. Gud hjälpe deras ande.
