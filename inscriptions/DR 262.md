## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Æsbiorn resþi sten þænsi æftiR "Dwærg, felaga sin, dræng goþan.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ásbjôrn reisti stein þenna eptir "Dverg, félaga sinn, dreng góðan.
```

## Translation to English

Ásbjôrn raised this stone in memory of Dvergr, his partner, a good valiant man.

## Translation to Swedish

Äsbjörn reste denna sten efter Dvärg, sin kamrat, en god ung man.
