## Normalization to Old West Norse


## Translation to English

Ásbjôrn and Gísl <aurir> and Auðbjôrn had the stone erected in memory of Bjôrn, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Åsbjörn och Gisl, <aurir> och Ödbjörn lät uppresa stenen efter Björn, sin fader.
