```text
sk(a)rþi + raisti + stain + þina × aftir + utryk + sun × sin + kuþ × hialbi + ant × hans × auk + harals
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian


## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Skarði reisti stein þenna eptir "Ótrygg, son sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans ok "Haralds.
```

## Translation to English

Skarði raised this stone in memory of Ótryggr, his son. May God help his spirit and Haraldr's.

## Translation to Swedish

Skarde reste denna sten efter Otrygg, sin son. Gud hjälpe hans ande och Haralds.
