```text
þ--kl + auk + kisl + lata + risa ' þina + stin · eftR · bruþur · sin sty(r)biun × auk sefari uiseti hiuk
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þ[or]kell ok "Gisl lata ræisa þenna stæin æftiR broður sinn "Styrbiorn, ok "Sæfari. "Viseti hiogg.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þ[or]kell ok "Gísl láta reisa þenna stein eptir bróður sinn "Styrbjôrn, ok "Sæfari. "Véseti hjó.
```

## Translation to English

Þorkell and Gísl have raised this stone in memory of their brother Styrbjôrn, and Sæfari (also raised). Véseti cut.

## Translation to Swedish

Torkel och Gisl låter resa denna sten efter sin broder Styrbjörn, och Säfare. Visäte högg.

## Other information

Nilsson (2001:14f) läser sefar ' vilket tolkas Sæfarr, Sævarr (mn) eller Sævar (kvn).
