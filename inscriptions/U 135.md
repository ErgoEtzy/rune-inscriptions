## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Ingifastr ok "Øystæinn ok "Svæinn letu ræisa stæina þessa at "Øystæin, faður sinn, ok bro þessa gærðu ok haug þenna.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ingifastr ok "Eysteinn ok "Sveinn létu reisa steina þessa at "Eystein, fôður sinn, ok brú þessa gerðu ok haug þenna.
```

## Translation to English

Ingifastr and Eysteinn and Sveinn had these stones raised in memory of Eysteinn, their father, and made this bridge and this mound.

## Translation to Swedish

Ingefast och Östen och Sven lät resa dessa stenar efter Östen, sin fader, och de gjorde denna bro och denna hög.
