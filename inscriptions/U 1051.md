## Normalization to Old West Norse


## Translation to English

Fastulfr had the stone erected in memory of his father-in-law and Jófast in memory of Vígmundr, her father.

## Translation to Swedish

Fastulv lät uppresa stenen efter sin svärfader, och Jofast efter Vigmund, sin fader.
