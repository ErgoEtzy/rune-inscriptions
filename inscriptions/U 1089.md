uihi ' lit raisa stain eftiR inkialt ' faþur ' sin iufurfas[t ']

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Vigi let ræisa stæin æftiR "Ingiald, faður sinn. "Iofurfast.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Vígi lét reisa stein eptir "Ingjald, fôður sinn. "Jôfurfast.
```

## Translation to English

Vígi had the stone raised in memory of Ingjaldr, his father. Jôfurfast.

## Translation to Swedish

Vige lät resa stenen efter Ingjald, sin fader. Jovurfast.
