## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Ingivaldr ok "Gislaug letu haggva stæina æftiR "Ingimund, sun sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ingivaldr ok "Gíslaug létu hôggva steina eptir "Ingimund, son sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ingivaldr and Gíslaug had the stones cut in memory of Ingimundr, their son.

## Translation to Swedish

Ingevald och Gislög lät hugga stenarna efter Ingemund, sin son.
