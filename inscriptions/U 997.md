
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ríkviðr lét reisa steina eptir "Ígul, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ríkviðr had the stones raised in memory of Ígull, his father.

## Translation to Swedish

Rikvid lät resa stenarna efter Igul, sin fader.
