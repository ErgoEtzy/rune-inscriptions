## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jóbjôrn/"Eybjôrn ok "Sveinn/"Steinn/"Seinn þeir reistu stein eptir "Þorfrøð, bónda "Ingiþóru.
```

## Translation to English

Jóbjôrn/Eybjôrn and Sveinn/Steinn/Seinn, they raised the stone in memory of Þorfrøðr, Ingiþóra's husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Jobjörn/Öbjörn och Sven/Sten de reste stenen efter Torfred, Ingetoras make.
