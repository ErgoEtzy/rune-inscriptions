## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Guðfastr let haggva stæina tva æftiR "Æist, sun sinn, ok sialfan sik.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Guðfastr lét hôggva steina tvá eptir "Eist, son sinn, ok sjalfan sik.
```

## Translation to English

Guðfastr had the two stones cut in memory of Eistr, his son, and for himself.

## Translation to Swedish

Gudfast lät hugga två stenar efter Est, sin son, och (efter) sig själv.
