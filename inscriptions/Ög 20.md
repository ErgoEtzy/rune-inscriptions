## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Holmgeirr ok "Brandi þeir reistu stein þenna eptir "Kjallak/"Kjullak, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Holmgeirr and Brandi, they raised this stone in memory of Kjallakr/Kjullakr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Holmger och Brande de reste denna sten efter Kjulak, sin fader.
