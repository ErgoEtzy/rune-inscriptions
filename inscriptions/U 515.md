## Transliteration

```text
kuni lit + stain + rita + eftiR × alfkiR faþur + sen + kuþ hialbi ant hans
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Gunni let stæin retta æftiR "AlfgæiR, faður sinn. "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gunni lét stein rétta eptir "Alfgeir, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Gunni had the stone erected in memory of Alfgeirr, his father. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Gunne lät resa stenen efter Alvger, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
