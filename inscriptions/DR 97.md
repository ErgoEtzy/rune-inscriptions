## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorwi, "Wigots kona, let resa sten þænsi æftiR "Þorbiorn, sun "Sibbu, systling sin, æs hon hugþi bætr þan swasum syni.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þyrvé, "Végauts kona, lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Þorbjôrn, son "Sibbu, systling sinn, er hon hugði betr en svásem syni.
```

## Translation to English

Þyrvé, Végautr's wife, had this stone raised in memory of Þorbjôrn, son of Sibba, her cousin, whom she cared for more than had he been her own son / than a dear son.
