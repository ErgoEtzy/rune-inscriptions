## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Ærnfastr ok "Ærngautr lata, syniR "SiggunnaR/"SigunnaR, ræisa stæin æftiR faður [sinn] "Kætilfast.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ernfastr ok "Erngautr láta, synir "Siggunnar/"Sigunnar, reisa stein eptir fôður [sinn] "Ketilfast.
```

## Translation to English

Ernfastr and Erngautr, Siggunnr's/Sigunnr's sons, have raised the stone in memory of their father Ketilfastr.

## Translation to Swedish

Ärnfast och Arngöt, Siggunns/Sigunns söner, låter resa stenen efter [sin] fader Kättilfast.
