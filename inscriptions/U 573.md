## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Alfgautr ok "Vígdjarfr létu reisa stein at fôður sinn "Víga.
```

## Translation to English

Alfgautr and Vígdjarfr had the stone raised in memory of their father Vígi.

## Translation to Swedish

Alvgöt och Vigdjärv lät resa stenen efter sin fader Vige.
