## Normalization to Old West Norse

## Translation to English

Ígulfastr and Faði and Fastulfr, they had these landmarks made in memory of Ôzurr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Igulfast och Fade och Fastulv, de lät göra dessa minnesmärken efter Assur, sin fader.
