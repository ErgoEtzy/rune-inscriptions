## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Tosti ok "Sigfuss ok "Sigmarr letu ræisa stæ[in] æftiR "Tobba/"Tubba, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Tosti ok "Sigfúss ok "Sigmarr létu reisa ste[in] eptir "Tobba/"Tubba, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Tosti and Sigfúss and Sigmarr had the stone raised in memory of Tobbi/Tubbi, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Toste och Sigfus och Sigmar lät resa stenen efter Tobbe/Tubbe, sin fader.
