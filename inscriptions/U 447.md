## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"HæmingR ok "Holmi ræistu stæin æftiR "<fiauar> broður sinn. "Guð hialpi þæim.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Hemingr ok "Holmi reistu stein eptir "<fiauar> bróður sinn. "Guð hjalpi þeim.
```

## Translation to English

Hemingr and Holmi raised the stone in memory of <fiauar>, their brother. May God help them.

## Translation to Swedish

Häming och Holme reste stenen efter <fiauar>, sin broder. Gud hjälpe dem.

## Other information
