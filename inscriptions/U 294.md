## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Guðlaug let stæin haggva æftiR "Forkunn, bonda sinn, ok "KætilælfR(?).
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Guðlaug lét stein hôggva eptir "Forkunn, bónda sinn, ok "Ketilelfr(?).
```

## Translation to English

Guðlaug had the stone cut in memory of Forkunnr, her husbandman; and Ketilelfr (also had it cut).

## Translation to Swedish

Gudlög lät hugga stenen efter Forkunn, sin make, och Kättilälv (lät också hugga).
