## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Skuli ok "Folki lata ræisa þennsa stæin æftiR broður sinn "Husbiorn/"Asbiorn. Hann vas siukR uti, þa þæiR giald toku a "Gutlandi.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Skúli ok "Folki láta reisa þenna stein eptir bróður sinn "Húsbjôrn/"Ásbjôrn. Hann var sjúkr úti, þá þeir gjald tóku á "Gotlandi.
```

## Translation to English

Skúli and Folki have raised this stone in memory of their brother Húsbjôrn/Ásbjôrn. He was sick abroad when they took payment on Gotland.

## Translation to Swedish

Skule och Folke låter resa denna sten efter sin broder Husbjörn. Han blev sjuk ute, då de tog gäld på Gotland.
