## Translation to English

Ônundr and Tosti, they erected the stone in memory of Áli/Alli, their father, and Gunnarr, their brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Anund och Toste, de reste stenen efter Åle/Alle, sin fader, och Gunnar, sin broder.
