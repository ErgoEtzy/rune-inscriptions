## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Eybjôrn ok "Þorgísl reistu stein þenna eptir "Ásleik, svær(?) sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Eybjôrn and Þorgísl raised this stone in memory of Ásleikr, their father/brother-in-law(?) May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Öbjörn och Torgils reste denna sten efter Åslek, sin svärfar(?). Gud hjälpe hans ande.
