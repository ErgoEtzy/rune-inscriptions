## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
§A "Sasgerðr reisti stein, "Finnulfs dóttir, at "Óðinkár "Ásbjarnar son, þann dýra ok hinn dróttinfasta. §B Síði sá maðr er þessi kuml of brjóti.
```

## Translation to English

§A Sasgerðr, Finnulfr's daughter, raised the stone, in memory of Óðinkárr Ásbjôrn's son, the valued and loyal to his lord. §B A sorcerer (be) the man who breaks this monument!
