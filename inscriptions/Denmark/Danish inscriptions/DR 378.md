## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ásbjôrn lét rétta stein eptir "Bótríðu, konu sína. "Guð létti ônd æ.
```

## Translation to English

Ásbjôrn had the stone erected in memory of Bótríða, his wife. May God ever relieve (her) spirit.
