
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Káta gerði kuml þessi eptir "Svein "Bôllungs son, bónda sinn. Sá var þegna fyrstr.
```

## Translation to English

Káta made these monuments in memory of Sveinn Bôllungr's son, her husbandman. He was first among þegns.

## Translation to Swedish

Kåta gjorde dessa minnesmärken efter Sven, Ballungs son, sin make, han var den främste bland tägnar.
