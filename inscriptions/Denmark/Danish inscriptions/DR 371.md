## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Guðmundr ok "Freybjôrn reistu stein eptir "Ásbjôrn fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi sálu hans.
```

## Translation to English

Guðmundr and Freybjôrn raised the stone in memory of Ásbjôrn their father. May God help his soul.
