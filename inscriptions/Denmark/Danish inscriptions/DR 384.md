## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorsten ok "Swen resþu æftiR "Alfwin/"Olfun, faþur sin, ok æftiR "Þorlak, broþur sin. "Guþ hialpi ond þeRa ok "G[u]þs moþiR.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorsteinn ok "Sveinn reistu eptir "Alfvin/"Ôlfun, fôður sinn, ok eptir "Þorlak, bróður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd þeira ok "G[u]ðs móðir.
```

## Translation to English

Þorsteinn and Sveinn raised in memory of Alfvin/Ôlfun, their father, and in memory of Þorlakr, their brother. May God and God's mother help their spirits.
