```text
"Faðir lét hôggva stein þenna eptir "Bjôrn, er skip átti með honum.
```

## Translation to English

Faðir had this stone cut in memory of Bjôrn, who owned a ship with him.

## Translation to Swedish

Fader lät hugga denna sten efter Björn, som ägde skepp med honom.
