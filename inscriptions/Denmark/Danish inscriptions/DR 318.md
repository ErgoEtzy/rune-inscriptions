## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Brandr gærþi kumbl þøsi æftiR "Gubba, faþur sin, ok "Efi, felagi "Gubba.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Brandr gerði kuml þessi eptir "Gubba, fôður sinn, ok "Efi, félagi "Gubba.
```

## Translation to English

Brandr made these monuments in memory of Gubbi, his father, and (so did) Efi, Gubbi's partner.

## Translation to Swedish

Brand gjorde dessa minnesmärken efter Gubbe, sin fader, och Eve, Gubbes kamrat (lät också resa?).
