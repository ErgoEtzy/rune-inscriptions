## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Hroðmundr ok "Guðmundr letu ræisa stæin þennsa æftiR "Sloru(?)/"Slyðru(?), faður sinn goðan. "Auðbiorn risti.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Hróðmundr ok "Guðmundr létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Slóru(?)/"Slyðru(?), fôður sinn góðan. "Auðbjôrn risti.
```

## Translation to English

Hróðmundr and Guðmundr had this stone raised in memory of Slóra(?)/Slyðru(?), their good father. Auðbjôrn carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Rodmund och Gudmund lät resa denna sten efter Slora(?)/Slydra(?), sin gode fader. Ödbjörn ristade.

## Other information
