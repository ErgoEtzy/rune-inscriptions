## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þóra lét reisa stein eptir "Gunna, bónda sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Þóra had the stone raised in memory of Gunni, her husbandman. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Tora lät resa stenen efter Gunne, sin man. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
