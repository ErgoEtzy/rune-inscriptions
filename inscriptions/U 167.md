## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ôzurr lét reisa stein at "Gunna, mág sinn. Hann var bróðir "Guðlaugar. "Fótr risti rúnar þessar.
```

## Translation to English

Ôzurr had the stone raised in memory of Gunni, his kinsman-by-marriage. He was Guðlaug's brother. Fótr carved these runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Assur lät resa stenen efter Gunne, sin frände. Han var Gudlögs broder. Fot ristade dessa runor.
