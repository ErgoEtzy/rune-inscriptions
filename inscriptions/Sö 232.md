## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Freysteinn ok "Ígull reistu stein at "Þorbjôrn, fôður sinn, ok "Þórir at bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Freysteinn and Ígull raised the stone in memory of Þorbjôrn, their father; and Þórir in memory of his brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Frösten och Igul reste stenen efter Torbjörn, sin fader, och Tore efter sin broder.
