## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"SægæiRR let haggva stæin at "Una/"Unna, sun sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sægeirr lét hôggva stein at "Una/"Unna, son sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Sægeirr had the stone cut in memory of Uni/Unni, his son.

## Translation to Swedish

Säger lät hugga stenen efter Une/Unne, sin son.
