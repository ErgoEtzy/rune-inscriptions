## Normalization to Old West Norse

## Translation to English

Holmsteinn raised this stone and made the bridge in memory of Myrr(?) (The Ant ?), his father, who lived in Jatunstaðir.

## Translation to Swedish

Holmsten reste denna sten och gjorde bro efter Myr(?), sin fader, som bodde i Jättingsstad.
