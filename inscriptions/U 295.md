## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"VigæiRR(?) let stæin haggva [æ]f[tiR] "Bersu/"Birsu, sun sinn. "Fa[st]laug let stæin haggva æftiR b[r]oður sinn "Fulluga.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Végeirr(?) lét stein hôggva [e]p[tir] "Bersu/"Birsu, son sinn. "Fa[st]laug lét stein hôggva eptir b[r]óður sinn "Fulluga.
```

## Translation to English

Végeirr(?) had the stone cut in memory of Bersa/Birsa his son; Fastlaug had the stone cut in memory of her brother Fullugi.

## Translation to Swedish

Viger lät hugga stenen efter Bärsa/Birsa, sin son. Fastlög lät hugga stenen efter sin broder Fulluge.
