
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"<olbusi> lét reisa stein þenna at "Ófeig, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

<olbusi> had this stone raised in memory of Ófeigr, his father.

## Translation to Swedish

<olbusi> lät resa denna sten efter Ofeg, sin fader.

## Other information

Runföljden [olb](u)si kan enligt Williams (1990:90) formellt tolkas som Ôl-Bosi.
