## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Kagr(?)/"Gagr(?) ok "Hákon létu hôggva stein ok brú gera eptir "Erngísl, fôður sinn góðan. "Guð hjalpi sálu hans. "Tíðkumi hjó stein þenna.
```

## Translation to English

Kagr(?)/Gagr(?) and Hákon had the stone cut and the bridge made in memory of Erngísl, their good father. May God help his soul. Tíðkumi cut this stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Kag(?)/Gag(?) och Håkon lät hugga stenen och göra bro efter Ärngisl, sin gode fader . Gud hjälpe hans själ. Tidkume högg denna sten.
