## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jón ok "Jôfur[f]ast létu reisa stein ok brú eptir "Víga, fôður, son "Eysteins. "Œpir rist[i]. "Bjôrn.
```

## Translation to English

Jón and Jôfurfast had the stone raised and the bridge (made) in memory of Vígi, (their) father, Eysteinn's son. Œpir carved. Bjôrn.

## Translation to Swedish

Jon och Jovurfast lät resa stenen och (göra) bron efter Vige, (sin) fader, Östens/Estens son. Öpir ristade. Björn.
