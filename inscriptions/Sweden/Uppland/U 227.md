## Transliteration

```text
× u(l)[k]il × lit × raisa × istain × iftiR × fraistain × bruþur isin × a[uk × k]u[ntru] × ifti sun sin ×
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Ulfkell let ræisa stæin æftiR "Frøystæin, broður sinn, ok "<kuntru> æftiR sun sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ulfkell lét reisa stein eptir "Freystein, bróður sinn, ok "<kuntru> eptir son sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ulfkell had the stone raised in memory of Freysteinn, his brother; and <kuntru> in memory of his son.

## Translation to Swedish

Ulvkel lät resa stenen efter Frösten, sin broder, och <kuntru> efter sin son.
