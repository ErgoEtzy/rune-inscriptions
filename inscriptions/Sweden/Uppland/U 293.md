```text
"Forkuðr ok "ÞoriR lata ræisa stæina þessa æftiR faður sinn "Kætil. "Guð hi[a]lpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Forkunnr ok "Þórir láta reisa steina þessa eptir fôður sinn "Ketil. "Guð hj[a]lpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Forkunnr and Þórir have raised these stones in memory of their father Ketill. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Forkunn och Tore låter resa dessa stenar efter sin fader Kättil. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
