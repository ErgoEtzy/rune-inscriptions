## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Rauðr ok "Biorn letu ræisa stæin æftiR mag sinn "Sighvat. "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Rauðr ok "Bjôrn létu reisa stein eptir mág sinn "Sighvat. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Rauðr and Bjôrn had the stone raised in memory of their kinsman-by-marriage Sighvatr. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Röd och Björn lät resa stenen efter sin frände Sigvat. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
