## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian


## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Harðgeirr ok "Þorsteinn, "Mungeirr ok "Ketilmundr létu reisa stein eptir "Ulf, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Harðgeirr and Þorsteinn (and) Mungeirr and Ketilmundr had the stone raised in memory of Ulfr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Hårdger och Torsten, Munger och Kättilmund lät resa stenen efter Ulv, sin fader.
