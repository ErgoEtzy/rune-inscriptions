
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Hjalmviðr ok "Þorbjôrn, "Gunnbjôrn ok "Halfdan, þeir reistu stein þenna eptir bróður sinn "Ulf ok fôður sinn "Bjôrn ok bróður sinn "Blákára(?).
```

## Translation to English

Hjalmviðr and Þorbjôrn (and) Gunnbjôrn and Halfdan, they raised this stone in memory of their brother Ulfr and their father Bjôrn and their brother Blákári(?)

## Translation to Swedish

Hjälmvid och Torbjörn, Gunnbjörn och Halvdan, de reste denna sten efter sin broder Ulv och sin fader Björn och sin broder Blåkåre.
