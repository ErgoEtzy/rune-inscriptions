## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Heðindís ok "Erndís þær létu reisa stein at "Ófeig, fôður sinn góðan. "Balli risti stein.
```

## Translation to English

Heðindís and Erndís, they had the stone raised in memory of Ófeigr, their good father. Balli carved the stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Hedendis och Ärndis, de lät resa stenen efter Ofeg, sin gode fader. Balle ristade stenen.
