## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ágoti(?) ok "Svarthôfði létu rétta stein eptir fôður sinn "Hjalmgeir.
```

## Translation to English

Ágoti(?) and Svarthôfði had the stone erected in memory of their father Hjalmgeirr.

## Translation to Swedish

Ågote(?) och Svarthövde lät resa stenen efter sin fader Hjälmger.
