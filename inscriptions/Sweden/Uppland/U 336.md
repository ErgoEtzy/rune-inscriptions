## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ulfr lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Ónæm, fôðurbróður sinn. Þeir bjoggu báðir í "Báristôðum.
```

## Translation to English

Ulfr had this stone raised in memory of Ónæmr, his father's brother. They both lived in Báristaðir.

## Translation to Swedish

Ulv lät resa denna sten efter Onäm, sin farbroder. De bodde båda i Borresta.
