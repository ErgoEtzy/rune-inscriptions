## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sveinn ok "Ígulfastr þeir stein réttu ok brú gerðu ept[i]r "Ígul, fôður sinn, ok "Áselfi, móður sína.
```

## Translation to English

Sveinn and Ígulfastr, they erected the stone and made the bridge in memory of Ígull, their father and Áselfr, their mother.

## Translation to Swedish

Sven och Igulfast, de uppreste stenen och gjorde bron efter Igul, sin fader, och Åsälv, sin moder.
