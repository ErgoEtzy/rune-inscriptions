```text
"Óðalfríðr/"Óðalfreðr reisti steina þessa e[p]tir "Styr, fôður sinn. "G[u]ð hjalpi ônd þeira. "Brandr hjó rún[a]r.
```

## Translation to English

Óðalfríðr/Óðalfreðr raised these stones in memory of Styrr, her/his father. May God help their spirits. Brandr cut the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Odalfrid reste dessa stenar efter Styr, sin fader. Gud hjälpe deras själ. Brand högg runorna.
