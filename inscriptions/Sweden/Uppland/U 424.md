## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gýi lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Tafsa(?)/"Lafsa(?), son sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Gýi had this stone raised in memory of Tafsi(?)/Lafsi(?), his son. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Gye lät resa denna sten efter Tafse(?)/Lafse(?), sin son. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
