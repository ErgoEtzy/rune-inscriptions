
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
§A "Nasi/"Nesi ok þeir brœðr reistu stein þenna §B eptir "Jarl, fôður sinn góðan, ok brú "Guðs þakka gerðu.
```

## Translation to English

§A Nasi/Nesi and his brothers raised this stone §B in memory of Jarl, their good father, and made the bridge as thanks to God.

## Translation to Swedish

Nase och hans bröder reste denna sten efter Jarl, sin gode fader, och de gjorde bron till Guds behag.
