## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ríkr lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Fasta, fôður sinn, ok þau "Gunnþrúðr. "Guð hjalpi hans sálu ok "Guðs móðir.
```

## Translation to English

Ríkr had this stone raised in memory of Fasti, his father, and Gunnþrúðr (also raised). May God and God's mother help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Rik lät resa denna sten efter Faste, sin fader, så ock Gunntrud. Gud hjälpe hans själ och Guds moder.
