## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Olof let ræisa stæin þenna æftiR "Svarthaufða, faður sinn, ok æftiR "Þorð, broður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ólôf lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Svarthôfða, fôður sinn, ok eptir "Þórð, bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

ólôf had this stone raised in memory of Svarthôfði, her father, and in memory of Þórðr, her brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Olov lät resa denna sten efter Svarthövde, sin fader, och efter Tord, sin broder.
