## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Holmfriðr ok "Folkvi letu ræisa stæin þenna æftiR "Þorstæin, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Holmfríðr ok "Folkvé létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Þorstein, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Holmfríðr and Folkvé had this stone raised in memory of Þorsteinn, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Holmfrid och Folkvi lät resa denna sten efter Torsten, sin fader.
