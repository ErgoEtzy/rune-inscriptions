## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Eygeirr ok "Freygeirr þeir létu reisa stein at "Gota, fôður sinn. "Gu[ð hjal]pi sál hans.
```

## Translation to English

Eygeirr and Freygeirr, they had the stone raised in memory of Goti, their father. May God help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Öger och Fröger, de lät resa stenen efter Gute, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans själ.

## Other information

Har utgjort ett monument tillsammans med U 723 och U 725.
