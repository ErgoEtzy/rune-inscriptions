## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Folkbjôrn lét reisa stein eptir "Nef, bróður sinn. Þat eru synir "Ásgeirs.
```

## Translation to English

Folkbjôrn had the stone raised in memory of Nefr, his brother. They are Ásgeirr's sons.

## Translation to Swedish

Folkbjörn lät resa stenen efter Näv, sin broder. Det är Åsgers söner.
