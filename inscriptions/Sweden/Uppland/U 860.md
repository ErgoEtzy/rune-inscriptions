## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Fastbjôrn ok "Þórunnr létu rétta stein þenna eptir "Ingifast, bónda sinn. "Guð hjalpi hans ônd ok "Guðs móðir.
```

## Translation to English

Fastbjôrn and Þórunnr had this stone erected in memory of Ingifastr, their husbandman. May God and God's mother help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Fastbjörn och Torunn lät uppresa denna sten efter Ingefast, sin man. Gud och Guds moder bjälpe hans ande.
