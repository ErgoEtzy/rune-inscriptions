## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
§A "<uitan> let ræisa stæin þennsa æftiR sun sinn ok "Karl æftiR broður sinn. §B "Svæinn risti runaR þessaR æftiR "Uddulf.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
§A "<uitan> lét reisa stein þenna eptir son sinn ok "Karl eptir bróður sinn. §B "Sveinn risti rúnar þessar eptir "Oddulf.
```

## Translation to English

§A <uitan> had this stone raised in memory of his son; and Karl in memory of his brother. §B Sveinn carved these runes in memory of Oddulfr.

## Translation to Swedish

<uitan> lät resa denna sten efter sin son och Karl efter sin broder. Sven ristade dessa runor efter Uddulv.
