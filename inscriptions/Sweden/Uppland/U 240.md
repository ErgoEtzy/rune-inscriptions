## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Dan ok "Huskarl ok "Svæinn ok "Holmfriðr, þaun møðgin letu retta stæin þenna æftiR "Halfdan, faður þæiRa "Dans, ok "Holmfriðr at boanda sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Danr ok "Húskarl ok "Sveinn ok "Holmfríðr, þau mœðgin létu rétta stein þenna eptir "Halfdan, fôður þeira "Dans, ok "Holmfríðr at bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Danr and Húskarl and Sveinn and Holmfríðr, the mother and (her) sons, had this stone erected in memory of Halfdan, the father of Danr and his brothers; and Holmfríðr in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Dan och Huskarl och Sven och Holmfrid, moder och söner, lät resa denna sten efter Halvdan, fader till Dan och hans bröder, och Holmfrid efter sin make.
