## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gillaug lét reisa stein at "Sigdjarf, fôður sinn, bónda "Holmfríðar. "Balli risti steina þess[a] báða.
```

## Translation to English

Gillaug had the stone raised in memory of Sigdjarfr, her father, Holmfríðr's husbandman. Balli carved both of these stones.

## Translation to Swedish

Gillög lät resa stenen efter Sigdjärv, sin fader, Holmfrids man. Balle ristade dessa båda stenar.
