## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Arnnjótr ok "Vígnjótr ok "Signjótr þeir létu reisa stein þenna ept[i]r "..., fôður sinn. "Þorfastr risti rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Arnnjótr and Vígnjótr and Signjótr, they had this stone raised in memory of ... their father. Þorfastr carved the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Arnnjut och Vignjut och Signjut, de lät resa denna sten efter ..., sin fader. Torfast ristade runorna.»
