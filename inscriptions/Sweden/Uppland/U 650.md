## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"UlfgæiRR ok "Halfdan þæiR letu ræisa stæin at "Sigræif, faður sinn. "Guð hialpi [and] hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ulfgeirr ok "Halfdan þeir létu reisa stein at "Sigreif, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi [ônd] hans.
```

## Translation to English

Ulfgeirr and Halfdan, they had this stone raised in memory of Sigreifr, their father. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Ulvger och Halvdan, de lät resa stenen efter Sigrev, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
