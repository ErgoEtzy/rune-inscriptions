## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ingigerðr/"Ingikárr lét reisa stein eptir "Styðing/"Stœðing, fôður sinn, ok "Þorkell lét gera merki at "Ingigunni, sværu sína.
```

## Translation to English

Ingigerðr/Ingikárr had the stone raised in memory of Styðingr/Stœðingr, her father; and Þorkell had the landmark made in memory of Ingigunn, his mother-in-law.

## Translation to Swedish

Ingegärd/Ingeger lät resa stenen efter Styding/Stöding, sin fader, och Torkel lät göra minnesmärket efter Ingegunn, sin svärmoder.
