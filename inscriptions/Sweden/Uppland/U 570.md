## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Aki ok "Svæinn þæiR letu ræisa stæin æftiR "Otrygga, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Áki ok "Sveinn þeir létu reisa stein eptir "Ótryggva, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Áki and Sveinn, they had the stone raised in memory of Ótryggvi, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Åke och Sven, de lät resa stenen efter Otrygge, sin fader.
