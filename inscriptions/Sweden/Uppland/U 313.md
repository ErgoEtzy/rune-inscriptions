## Transliteration

```text
§A kunar lit hkua · stain · eftiR iafa^ur^k^ut^fast · a^s^bt^uiubu sina · auk hulmtis eftiR §B totur sina
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
§A "Gunnarr let haggva stæin æftiR "Iufurfast, stiupu sina, ok "Holmdis æftiR §B dottur sina.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
§A "Gunnarr lét hôggva stein eptir "Jôfurfast, stjúpu sína, ok "Holmdís eptir §B dóttur sína.
```

## Translation to English

§A Gunnarr had the stone cut in memory of Jôfurfast, his stepdaughter; and Holmdís in memory of §B her daughter.

## Translation to Swedish

Gunnar lät hugga stenen efter Jovurfast, sin styvdotter, och Holmdis efter sin dotter.
