## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ketilvé lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Ketil, fôður sinn, ok "Gyríðr eptir bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ketilvé had this stone raised in memory of Ketill, her father; and Gyríðr in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Kättilvi lät resa denna sten efter Kättil, sin fader, och Gyrid efter sin man.
