## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"GæiRbiorn ok "Iofurbiorn ok "Vifastr þæiR ræistu stæin þenna æftiR "GæiRbiorn, broður sinn. "Guð hialpi hans and ok salu.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Geirbjôrn ok "Jôfurbjôrn ok "Véfastr þeir reistu stein þenna eptir "Geirbjôrn, bróður sinn. "Guð hjalpi hans ônd ok sálu.
```

## Translation to English

Geirbjôrn and Jôfurbjôrn and Véfastr, they raised this stone in memory of Geirbjôrn, their brother. May God help his spirit and soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Gerbjörn och Jovurbjörn och Vifast, de reste denna sten efter Gerbjörn, sin broder. Gud hjälpe hans ande och själ.
