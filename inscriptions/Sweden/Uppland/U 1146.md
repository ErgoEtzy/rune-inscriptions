## Normalization to Old West Norse


## Translation to English

Ráðulfr and Fundinn and Ônundr, the brothers erected this stone in memory of Kári the Eloquent, their father, the son of Uggr of Svanabýr.

## Translation to Swedish

Rådulv och Funnen och Anund, bröderna, uppreste denna sten efter Kåre, sin fader, den målspake, son till Ugg i Svanby.
