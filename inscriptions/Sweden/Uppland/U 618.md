## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Ingiriðr ok "Gyrið letu retta stæin æftiR "Klemit, faður sinn, ok "Ingigærðr(?) [at] boanda sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ingríðr ok "Gyríðr létu rétta stein eptir "Klemet, fôður sinn, ok "Ingigerðr(?) [at] bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ingríðr and Gyríðr had the stone erected in memory of Klemet, their father, and Ingigerðr(?) in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Ingrid och Gyrid lät resa stenen efter Klemet, sin fader, och Ingegärd (?) efter sin man.
