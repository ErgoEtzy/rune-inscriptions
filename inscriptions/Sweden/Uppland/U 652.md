```text
"Gunnhildr let gærva mærki þessa at "Þialf[a], sun sinn, ok æftiR sik sialfa. "Arbiorn hiogg stæin þennsa.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gunnhildr lét gera merki þessi at "Þjalf[a], son sinn, ok eptir sik sjalfa. "Arnbjôrn hjó stein þenna.
```

## Translation to English

Gunnhildr had these landmarks made in memory of Þjalfi, her son, and in memory of herself. Arnbjôrn cut this stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Gunnhild lät göra dessa minnesmärken efter Tjälve, sin son, och efter sig själv. Arbjörn högg denna sten.
