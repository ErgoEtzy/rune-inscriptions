## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Óleifr lét brú gera ok "Sveinn [at "K]arl ok "Heimlaug(?) lét hôggva stein eptir "Karl, bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Óleifr and Sveinn had the bridge made in memory of Karl; and Heimlaug had the stone cut in memory of Karl, her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Olev lät göra bron och Sven [efter K]arl, och Hemlög(?) lät hugga stenen efter Karl, sin make.
