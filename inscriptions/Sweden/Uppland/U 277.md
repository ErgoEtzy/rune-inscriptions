## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian


## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ketilgerðr lét stein hôggva eptir "Ôzur, bónda sinn, ok "Ulfr lét hôggva eptir "Bjôrn.
```

## Translation to English

Ketilgerðr had the stone cut in memory of Ôzurr, her husbandman; and Ulfr had (it) cut in memory of Bjôrn.

## Translation to Swedish

Kättilgärd lät hugga stenen efter Assur, sin make, och Ulv lät hugga efter Björn.
