## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"OlafR ok "Gunnhildr letu ræisa stæin æftiR "Gunna, sun sinn, ok "Kætill let æftiR broður sinn. "ØpiR risti runaR.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ólafr ok "Gunnhildr létu reisa stein eptir "Gunna, son sinn, ok "Ketill lét eptir bróður sinn. "Œpir risti rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Ólafr and Gunnhildr had the stone raised in memory of Gunni, their son; and Ketill had (it raised) in memory of his brother. Œpir carved the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Olov och Gunnhild lät resa stenen efter Gunne, sin son, och Kättil lät (resa stenen) efter sin broder. Öpir ristade runorna.
