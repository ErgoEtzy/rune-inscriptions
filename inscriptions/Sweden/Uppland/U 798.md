```text
[uibiarn × raisti × stain × iftir × katil + kuþ hialbi + ant hans]
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Vibiorn ræisti stæin æftiR "Kætil. "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Vébjôrn reisti stein eptir "Ketil. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Vébjôrn raised the stone in memory of Ketill. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Vibjörn reste stenen efter Kättil. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
