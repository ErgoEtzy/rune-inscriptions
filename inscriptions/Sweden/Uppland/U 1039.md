## Translation to English

§AP Stóðbjôrn(?) and Eysteinn erected the stone in memory of their father Gulli(?). May Christ have mercy(?), relieve his soul. §AQ Stóðbjôrn(?) and Eysteinn erected the stone in memory of their father Gulli Myskja. May Christ relieve his soul. §B Kjúli(?) carved these runes. His wife was called Ketillaug/Ketillauga. And Véelfr.
