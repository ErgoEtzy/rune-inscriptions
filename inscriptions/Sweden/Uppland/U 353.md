## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Soni rétti stein ok gerði brú eptir "Sigbjôrn ok "Þegn. "Soni risti.
```

## Translation to English

Soni erected the stone and made the bridge in memory of Sigbjôrn and Þegn. Soni carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Sune reste stenen och gjorde bron efter Sigbjörn och Tjägn. Sune ristade.
