## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Inga let ræisa stæina ok bro gærva æftiR "Ragnfast, bonda sinn. "Assurr vaR huskarl hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Inga lét reisa steina ok brú gera eptir "Ragnfast, bónda sinn. "Ôzurr var húskarl hans.
```

## Translation to English

Inga had the stones raised and the bridge made in memory of Ragnfastr, her husbandman. Ôzurr was his housecarl.

## Translation to Swedish

»Inga lät resa stenarna och göra bron efter Ragnfast, sin make. Assur var hans huskarl.
