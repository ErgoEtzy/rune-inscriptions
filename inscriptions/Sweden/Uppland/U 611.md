## Normalization to Old West Norse


## Translation to English

Bjôrn and Steinfríðr had the stone raised in memory of Gísli. He fell abroad in Freygeirr's(?) retinue.

## Translation to Swedish

Björn och Stenfrid lät resa stenen efter Gisle. Han föll utomlands i Frögers(?) följe.
