## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Svarthaufði let ræisa stæin þennsa æftiR "Anund, sun sinn. "Guð hialpi and hans ok allum kristnum.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Svarthôfði lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Ônund, son sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans ok ôllum kristnum.
```

## Translation to English

Svarthôfði had this stone raised in memory of Ônund, his son. May God help his and all Christians' spirits.

## Translation to Swedish

Svarthövde lät resa denna sten efter Anund, sin son. Gud hjälpe hans ande och alla kristna.

## Other information

U 457-U 459 härrör från samma ätt.
