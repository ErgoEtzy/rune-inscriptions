## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Snorrir/"Snerrir ok "Krókr reistu stein þenna eptir "Herjulf, eptir fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Snorrir/Snerrir and Krókr raised this stone in memory of Herjulfr, in memory of their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Snorre och Krok reste denna sten efter Härjulv, efter sin fader.
