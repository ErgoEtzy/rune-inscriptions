## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jôrundr ok "Brynjulfr, "Ingifastr létu reisa stein at "Ásbjôrn, fôður sinn. "Œpir risti.
```

## Translation to English

Jôrundr and Brynjulfr (and) Ingifastr had the stone raised in memory of Ásbjôrn, their father. Œpir carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Jorund och Brynjulv, Ingefast lät resa stenen efter Åsbjörn, sin fader. Öpir ristade.
