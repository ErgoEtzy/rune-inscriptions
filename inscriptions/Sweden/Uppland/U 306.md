## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ingríðr lét hôggva stein eptir "Jóbjôrn, ok "Ingimarr ok "Ingivaldr ok "Karl ok "Ingríðr.
```

## Translation to English

Ingríðr had the stone cut in memory of Jóbjôrn, and Ingimarr and Ingivaldr and Karl and Ingríðr (also had it cut).

## Translation to Swedish

Ingrid lät hugga stenen efter Jobjörn, och Ingemar och Ingevald och Karl och Ingrid.
