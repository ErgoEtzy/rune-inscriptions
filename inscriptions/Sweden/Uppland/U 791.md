## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Freysteinn lét hôggva stein eptir "Eistulf, son sinn, ok brú gera.
```

## Translation to English

Freysteinn had the stone cut and the bridge made in memory of Eistulfr, his son.

## Translation to Swedish

Frösten lät hugga stenen efter Estulv, sin son, och göra bron.
