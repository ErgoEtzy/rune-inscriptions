## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jôrundr reisti steina þ[essa epti]r "Jarl, son sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Jôrundr raised these stones in memory of Jarl, his son. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Järund reste dessa stenar efter Jarl, sin son. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
