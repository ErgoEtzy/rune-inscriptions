## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Hælgi let haggva s[t]æin þennsa at "Salmund, sun sinn. "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Helgi lét hôggva s[t]ein þenna at "Salmund, son sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Helgi had this stone cut in memory of Salmundr, his son. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Helge lät hugga denna sten efter Salmund, sin son. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
