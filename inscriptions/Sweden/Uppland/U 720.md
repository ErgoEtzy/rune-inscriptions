## Normalization to Old West Norse


## Translation to English

Myndill and Farbjôrn and Arngeirr and <kigumantr> had this stone raised in memory of Japr(?)/Jarp(?), their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Myndel och Farbjörn och Arnger och <kigumantr> lät resa denna sten efter Jap(?)/Jarp(?), sin fader.
