## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Brúsi ok "Þorbjôrn létu reisa stein eptir fôður sinn "B[j]ôrn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Brúsi and Þorbjôrn had the stone raised in memory of their father Bjôrn. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Bruse och Torbjörn lät resa stenen efter sin fader Björn. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
