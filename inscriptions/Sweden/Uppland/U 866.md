## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Krókr lét rétta stein þenna eptir "Kára, fô[ður sinn. "Guð] hjalpi hans sálu ok "Guðs móðir.
```

## Translation to English

Krókr had this stone erected in memory of Kári, his father. May God and God's mother help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Krok lät resa denna sten efter Kåre, sin fader. Gud och Guds moder hjälpe hans själ.
