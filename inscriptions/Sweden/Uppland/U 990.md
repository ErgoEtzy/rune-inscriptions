## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Veðr ok "Þegn ok "Gunnarr reistu stein þenna at "Haursa, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Veðr and Þegn and Gunnarr raised this stone in memory of Haursi, their father. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Väder och Tägn och Gunnar reste denna sten efter Hörse, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
