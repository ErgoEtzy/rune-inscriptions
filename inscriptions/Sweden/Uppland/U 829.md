## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Forkunnr ok "Gunnarr þæiR letu haggva stæin þennsa at "Anund, faður sinn, boanda "AuðfriðaR. "Balli risti stæin þennsa.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Forkunnr ok "Gunnarr þeir létu hôggva stein þenna at "Ônund, fôður sinn, bónda "Auðfríðar. "Balli risti stein þenna.
```

## Translation to English

Forkunnr and Gunnarr, they had this stone cut in memory of Ônundr, their father, Auðfríðr's husbandman. Balli carved this stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Forkunn och Gunnar, de lät hugga denna sten efter Anund, sin fader, Ödfrids man. Balle ristade denna sten.
