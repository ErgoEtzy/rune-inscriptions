## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"ÞolfR ok "FastulfR ok "ÆrnlæifR þæiR letu haggva stæin at "Hrok, faður sinn. "Balli risti stæin.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þólfr ok "Fastulfr ok "Ernleifr þeir létu hôggva stein at "Hrók, fôður sinn. "Balli risti stein.
```

## Translation to English

Þólfr and Fastulfr and Ernleifr, they had the stone cut in memory of Hrókr, their father. Balli carved the stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Torulv och Fastulv och Ärnlev, de lät hugga stenen efter Rok, sin fader. Balle ristade stenen.
