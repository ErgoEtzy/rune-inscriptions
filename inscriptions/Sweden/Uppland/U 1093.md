## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Fullugi ok "Ásgunnr þau hjón létu hôgg[v]a stein eptir sik kvik bæði. "Guð hjalpi ônd.
```

## Translation to English

Fullugi and Ásgunnr, this married couple had the stone cut in memory of themselves while both alive. May God help (their) spirits.

## Translation to Swedish

Fulluge och Åsgunn, dessa makar, lät hugga stenen efter sig båda, medan de levde. Gud hjälpe anden.
