## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Vígdjarfr ok "Jôrundr ok "Sigbjôrn létu reisa stein eptir "Svartung, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Vígdjarfr and Jôrundr and Sigbjôrn had the stone raised in memory of Svartungr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Vigdjärv och Jorund och Sigbjörn lät resa stenen efter Svartung, sin fader.
