## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian


## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Heðingeirr ok "Bjôrn reistu eptir "Gilda(?), bónda góðan.
```

## Translation to English

Heðingeirr and Bjôrn raised (this) in memory of Gildi(?), the good husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Hedenger och Björn reste (stenen) efter Gilde(?), en god bonde.
