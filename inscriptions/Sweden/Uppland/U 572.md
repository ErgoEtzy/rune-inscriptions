## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"...laug let bro gærva, stæin retta æftiR "Ioan, boanda sinn, ok "Gunnarr ok "Ali/"Alli æftiR broður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"...laug lét brú gera, stein rétta eptir "Jóhan, bónda sinn, ok "Gunnarr ok "Áli/"Alli eptir bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

...-laug had the bridge made (and) the stone erected in memory of Jóhan, her husbandman; and Gunnarr and Áli/Alli in memory of their brother.

## Translation to Swedish

…-lög lät göra bron och resa stenen efter Johan, sin man, och Gunnar och Åle/Alle efter sin broder.
