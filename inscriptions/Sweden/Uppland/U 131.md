## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þiagn ok "Dan letu ræisa stæin æftiR "Þorstæin, faður sinn, ok "Gunnvar æftiR bonda sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þegn ok "Danr létu reisa stein eptir "Þorstein, fôður sinn, ok "Gunnvôr eptir bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þegn and Danr had the stone raised in memory of Þorsteinn, their father; and Gunnvôr in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Tägn och Dan lät resa stenen efter Torsten, sin fader, och Gunnvar efter sin make.
