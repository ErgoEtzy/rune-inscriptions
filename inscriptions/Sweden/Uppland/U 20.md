## Translation to English

... Eiríkr(?) his/her husbandman ... and ... Guðríkr, his/her father. May God help (his) spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

... Erik(?), sin make ... och ... Gudrík, sin fader. Gud hjälpe anden.
