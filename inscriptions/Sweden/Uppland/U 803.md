## Transliteration

```text
ulfr : lit : raesa : istaen siulfr : han : yiftiR : sik
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"UlfR let ræisa stæin, sialfR hann æftiR sik.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ulfr lét reisa stein, sjalfr hann eptir sik.
```

## Translation to English

Ulfr had the stone raised, he himself in memory of himself.

## Translation to Swedish

Ulv lät resa stenen, han själv efter sig.

## Other information

Ristningen har i sen tid "förbättrats" av en stenhuggare.
