## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Nesbjôrn ok "Rúna, "Ônundar dóttir, létu rétta steina at "<suhikierf>, son sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Nesbjôrn and Rúna, Ônundr's daughter, had the stones erected in memory of <suhikierf>, their son.

## Translation to Swedish

Näsbjörn och Runa, Anunds dotter, lät uppresa stenarna efter <suhik'ierf>f, sin son.
