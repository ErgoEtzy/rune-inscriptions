## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Siglaug let stæina retta æftiR "Sigvið, sun sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Siglaug lét steina rétta eptir "Sigvið, son sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Siglaug had the stones erected in memory of Sigviðr, her son.

## Translation to Swedish

Siglög lät uppresa stenen efter Sigvid, sin son.
