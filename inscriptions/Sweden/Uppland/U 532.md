## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Sigruð let ræisa stæin æftiR "Kara, bonda sinn. "Guð hialpi and hans. "Þorbiorn "Skald hiogg runaR.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sigþrúðr lét reisa stein eptir "Kára, bónda sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans. "Þorbjôrn "Skald hjó rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Sigþrúðr had the stone raised in memory of Kári, her husbandman. May God help his spirit. Þorbjôrn Skald cut the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Sigrud lät resa stenen efter Kåre, sin man. Gud hjälpe hans ande. Torbjörn skald högg runorna.
