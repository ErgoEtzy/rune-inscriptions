## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Biorn, "FinnviðaR sunn, let ræisa stæin æftiR sik sialfan.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Bjôrn, "Finnviðar sonr, lét reisa stein eptir sik sjalfan.
```

## Translation to English

Bjôrn, Finnviðr's son, had the stone raised in memory of himself.

## Translation to Swedish

Björn, Finnvids son, lät resa stenen efter sig själv.
