## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sigbjôrn/"Sæbjôrn ok "Sigdjarfr réttu stein þenna eptir "Eystein ok eptir "Sigdjarf/"Sædjarf, brœðr sína. Synir "Hugals/"Hugalds réttu stein þenna.
```

## Translation to English

Sigbjôrn/Sæbjôrn and Sigdjarfr erected this stone in memory of Eysteinn and in memory of Sigdjarfr/Sædjarfr, their brothers. Hugall's/Hugaldr's sons erected this stone.

## Translation to Swedish

»Sigbjörn/Säbjörn och Sigdjärv uppreste denna sten efter Östen/Esten och efter Sigdjärv/Sädjärv, sina bröder. Hugals/Hugalds söner uppreste denna sten.
