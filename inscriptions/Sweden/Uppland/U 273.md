## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Illugi lét stein rétta [at "Djarf], fôður sinn, "Ôzurr ok "Fullugi at bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Illugi had the stone erected in memory of Djarfr, his father; Ôzurr and Fullugi in memory of their brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Illuge lät resa stenen [efter Djärv], sin fader, Assur och Fulluge efter sin broder.
