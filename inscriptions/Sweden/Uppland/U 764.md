## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Bruni ok "Holmstæinn letu ræisa stæin þennsa æftiR "Gunnbiorn, faður sinn goðan.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Brúni ok "Holmsteinn létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Gunnbjôrn, fôður sinn góðan.
```

## Translation to English

Brúni and Holmsteinn had this stone raised in memory of Gunnbjôrn, their good father.

## Translation to Swedish

Brune och Holmsten lät resa denna sten efter Gunnbjörn, sin gode fader.
