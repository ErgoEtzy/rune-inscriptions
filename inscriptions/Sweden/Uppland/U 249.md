## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gunnviðr lét stein hôggva ok "Kasi/"Kassi/"Gási eptir "Svarthôfða, fôður sinn, mann g[óð]an(?).
```

## Translation to English

Gunnviðr had the stone cut, and Kasi/Kassi/Gási (too) in memory of Svarthôfði, their father, a good(?) man.

## Translation to Swedish

Gunnvid lät hugga stenen och Kase/Kasse/Gåse efter Svarthövde, sin fader, en god man.
