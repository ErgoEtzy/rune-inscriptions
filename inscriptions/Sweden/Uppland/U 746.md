## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Vigisl let ræisa stæin æftiR sun sinn "Sigræif. "Þorgautr risti stæin þenna.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Végísl lét reisa stein eptir son sinn "Sigreif. "Þorgautr risti stein þenna.
```

## Translation to English

Végísl had the stone raised in memory of his son Sigreifr. Þorgautr carved this stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Vigisl lät resa stenen efter sin son Sigrev. Torgöt ristade denna sten.
