## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Hôsvi ok "Sigríðr ok "Ketiley létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Holmstein, bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Hôsvi and Sigríðr and Ketiley had this stone raised in memory of Holmsteinn, their brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Hosve och Sigrid och Kättilö lät resa denna sten efter Holmsten, sin broder.
