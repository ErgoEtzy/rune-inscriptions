## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þora let ræisa stæin þenna at "Agmund ok æftiR "Sibba, sun sinn. "Arbiorn hiogg stæin.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þóra lét reisa stein þenna at "Agmund ok eptir "Sibba, son sinn. "Arnbjôrn hjó stein.
```

## Translation to English

Þóra had this stone raised in memory of Agmundr and in memory of Sibbi, her son. Arnbjôrn cut the stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Tora lät resa denna sten efter Agmund och efter Sibbe, sin son. Arbjörn högg stenen.
