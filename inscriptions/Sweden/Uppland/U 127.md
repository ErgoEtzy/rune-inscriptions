## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Iarlabanki let ræisa stæina þessa at sik kvikvan, ok bro þessa gærði fyr and sina, ok æinn atti "Tæby alla[n].
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jarlabanki lét reisa steina þessa at sik kvikvan, ok brú þessa gerði fyrir ônd sína, ok einn átti "Tábý alla[n].
```

## Translation to English

Jarlabanki had these stones raised in memory of himself while alive, and made this bridge for his spirit, and (he) alone owned all of Tábýr.

## Translation to Swedish

Jarlabanke lät resa dessa stenar efter sig, medan han levde, och han gjorde denna bro för sin själ, och han ägde ensam hela Täby.
