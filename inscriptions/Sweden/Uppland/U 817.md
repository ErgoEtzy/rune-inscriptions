## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Auðgeirr/"Auðgerðr ok "Ágeirr/"Ágerðr létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Gulla, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Auðgeirr/Auðgerðr and Ágeirr/Ágerðr had this stone raised in memory of Gulli, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Ödger och Åger lät resa denna sten efter Gulle, sin fader.
