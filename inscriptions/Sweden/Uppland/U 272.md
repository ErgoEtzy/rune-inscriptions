## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Iofurfast l[et ræisa] stæin ok bro gærva [æfti]R "... sun sinn. "Guð hialpin sialu hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jôfurfast l[ét reisa] stein ok brú gera [epti]r "... son sinn. "Guð hjalpi sálu hans.
```

## Translation to English

Jôfurfast had the stone raised and the bridge made in memory of ... his son. May God help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Jovurfast lät [resa] stenen och göra bron efter ..., sin son. Gud hjälpe hans själ.
