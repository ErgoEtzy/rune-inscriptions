## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"ÆilifR ok "Sigviðr letu ræisa stæin æftiR "Vifast, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Eilífr ok "Sigviðr létu reisa stein eptir "Véfast, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Eilífr and Sigviðr had the stone raised in memory of Véfastr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Eliv och Sigvid lät resa stenen efter Vifast, sin fader.
