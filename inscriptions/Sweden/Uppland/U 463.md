## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Veðraldi ok "Vígi létu reisa stein eptir "Holmstein, fôður sinn, ok "Holmfríðr at bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Veðraldi and Vígi had the stone raised in memory of Holmsteinn, their father; and Holmfríðr in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Vädralde och Vige lät resa stenen efter Holmsten, sin fader, och Holmfrid efter sin man.
