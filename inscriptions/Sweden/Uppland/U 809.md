## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Bjôrn ok "Gísl reistu stein þenna eptir "Andvétt, fôður sinn; bjó í "Hervistôðum, frænda "Gerðars.
```

## Translation to English

Bjôrn and Gísl raised this stone in memory of Andvéttr, their father, Gerðarr's kinsman (who) lived in Hervistaðir.

## Translation to Swedish

Björn och Gisl reste denna sten efter Andvätt, sin fader; bodde på Härvesta, Gärdars frände.
