## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Forkuðr ok "Fullhugi letu rista runaR æftiR "Illhuga, faður sinn. "Kristr hialpin. "Ærinfastr risti.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Forkunnr ok "Fullugi létu rista rúnar eptir "Illuga, fôður sinn. "Kristr hjalpi. "Erinfastr risti.
```

## Translation to English

Forkunnr and Fullugi had the runes carved in memory of Illugi, their father. May Christ help. Erinfastr carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Forkunn och Fulluge lät rista runorna efter Illuge, sin fader. Krist hjälpe. Ärenfast ristade.
