## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ófeigr ok "...bjôrn þeir létu reisa steina þessa at "Ernbjôrn, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi sál hans.
```

## Translation to English

Ófeigr and ...-bjôrn, they had these stones raised in memory of Ernbjôrn, their father. May God help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Ofeg och ...-björn de lät resa dessa stenar efter Ärnbjörn, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans själ.
