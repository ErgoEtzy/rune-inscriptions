## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ingifastr lét reisa stein eptir "Óleif, fôður sinn, ok "Eyðr eptir bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ingifastr had the stone raised in memory of Óleifr, his father; and Eyðr in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Ingefast lät resa stenen efter Olev, sin fader, och Öd efter sin man.
