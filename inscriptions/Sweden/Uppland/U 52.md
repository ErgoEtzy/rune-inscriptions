## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Áfríðr lét at "Svarthôfða ok at "Ígulfast, sonu sína, ok at "Ásgaut.
```

## Translation to English

Áfríðr had (this cut) in memory of Svarthôfði and in memory of Ígulfastr, her sons, and in memory of Ásgautr.

## Translation to Swedish

Åfrid lät (hugga hällen) efter Svarthövde och efter Igulfast, sina söner, och efter Åsgöt.
