## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ulfketill ok "Gýi ok "Uni/"Unni þeir létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Ulf, fôður sinn góðan. Hann bjó í "Skolhamri. "Guð hjalpi hans ônd ok sálu ok "Guðs móðir, lé honum ljós ok paradís.
```

## Translation to English

Ulfketill and Gýi and Uni/Unni, they had this stone raised in memory of Ulfr, their good father. He lived in Skolhamarr. May God and God's mother help his spirit and soul; grant him light and paradise.

## Translation to Swedish

Ulvkättil och Gye och Une/Unne de lät resa denna sten efter Ulv, sin gode fader. Han bodde i Skålhamra. Gud och Guds moder hjälpe hans ande och själ, förläne honom ljus och paradis.
