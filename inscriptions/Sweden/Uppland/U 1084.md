## Normalization to Old West Norse

"Ábjôrn ok "Ingjaldr ok "Hemingr létu rétta stein eptir "Bjôrn, fôður sinn.

## Translation to English

Ábjôrn and Ingjaldr and Hemingr had the stone erected in memory of Bjôrn, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Åbjörn och Ingjald och Häming lät resa stenen efter Björn, sin fader.
