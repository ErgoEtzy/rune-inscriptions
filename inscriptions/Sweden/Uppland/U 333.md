## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ósníkinn ok "Signjótr ok "Sigviðr láta reisa stein eptir "Brúsa, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ósníkinn and Signjótr and Sigviðr have raised the stone in memory of Brúsi, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Osniken och Signjut och Sigvid låter resa stenen efter Bruse, sin fader.
