## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ketilbjôrn ok "Fasta létu rétta stein eptir fôður sinn, "Ernfastr.
```

## Translation to English

Ketilbjôrn and Fasta had the stone erected in memory of their father Ernfastr.

## Translation to Swedish

Kättilbjörn och Fasta lät uppresa stenen efter sin fader Ärnfast.
