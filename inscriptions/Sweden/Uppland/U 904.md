## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jarl ok "Karl ok "Ígulbjôrn létu rétta steina þessa ok gera brú þessa eptir "Jôfur, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Jarl and Karl and Ígulbjôrn had these stones erected and this bridge made in memory of Jôfurr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Jarl och Karl och Igulbjörn lät resa dessa stenar och göra denna bro efter Jovur, sin fader.
