## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jarlaba[nki] lét reisa steina þessa at sik kvikvan, ok brú þessa gerði fyrir ônd sína, ok einn átti allan "Tábý.
```

## Translation to English

Jarlabanki had these stones raised in memory of himself while alive and made this bridge for his spirit, and (he) alone owned all of Tábýr.

## Translation to Swedish

Jarlabanke lät resa dessa stenar efter sig, medan han levde, och han gjorde denna bro för sin själ, och ensam ägde han hela Täby.
