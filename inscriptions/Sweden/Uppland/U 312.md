## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gunnarr, sonr "Farulfs, lét gera merki fyrir sál "Jôfurfastar, stjúpu sinnar, dóttur "Holmdísar.
```

## Translation to English

Gunnarr, Farulfr's son, had the landmark made for the soul of Jôfurfast, his stepdaughter, Holmdís' daughter.

## Translation to Swedish

Gunnar, Farulvs son, lät göra minnesmärket för Jovurfasts själ, sin styvdotters, Holmdis' dotters.
