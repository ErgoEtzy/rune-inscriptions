## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Fastbjôrn ok "Þórunnr létu rétta ... brú gera eptir "Ingifa[st, b]ónda sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd. "Ásmundr risti rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Fastbjôrn and Þórunnr had ... erected ... the bridge made in memory of Ingifastr, their husbandman. May God help his spirit. Ásmundr carved the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Fastbjörn och Torunn lät uppresa [denna sten och] göra bron efter lngefast, sin man. Gud hjälpe anden. Åsmund ristade runorna.
