## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Halfdan ok "Signjótr þeir létu reisa stein at "Auðgeir ok "Dan, feðr sína, ok at "Almgeir ok "Vébjôrn, brœðr "Halfdanar.
```

## Translation to English

Halfdan and Signjótr, they had the stone raised in memory of Auðgeirr and Danr, their fathers, and in memory of Almgeirr and Vébjôrn, Halfdan's brothers.

## Translation to Swedish

Halvdan och Signjut, de lät resa stenen efter Ödger och Dan, sina fäder, och efter Almger och Vibjörn, Halvdans bröder.
