## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Brúni lét reisa ok "Arnvé(?) stein þenna eptir "Guðfast, fôður "Brúna, ok "Arnvé(?) eptir bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Brúni and Arnvé(?) had this stone raised in memory of Guðfastr, Brúni's father; and Arnvé(?) in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Brune lät resa och Are(?)/Arnvi(?) denna sten efter Gudfast, Brunes fader, och Are(?)/Arnvi(?) efter sin man.
