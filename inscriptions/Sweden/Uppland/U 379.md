## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
Frísa gildar létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Þor[kel, gild]a sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans. "Þorbjôrn risti.
```

## Translation to English

The Frisian guild-brothers had this stone raised in memory of Þorkell, their guild-brother. May God help his spirit. Þorbjôrn carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Frisernas gillebröder lät resa denna sten efter Torkel, sin gillebroder. Gud hjälpe hans ande. Torbjörn ristade.
