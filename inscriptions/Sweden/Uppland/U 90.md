## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Véfríðr lét hôggva helli þessa eptir bróður sinn "Þerf, ok "Mundi at mág sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Véfríðr had this rock-slab cut in memory of his brother Þerfr; and Mundi in memory of his kinsman-by-marriage.

## Translation to Swedish

Vifrid lät hugga denna häll efter sin broder Tärv, och Munde efter sin svåger.
