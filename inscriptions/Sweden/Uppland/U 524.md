## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gulli ok "Stóðkell reistu stein eptir "Viðbjôrn, arfa "Krums góðan. "Viðbjôrn hjó.
```

## Translation to English

Gulli and Stóðkell raised the stone in memory of Viðbjôrn, the good heir of Krumr. Viðbjôrn cut.

## Translation to Swedish

Gulle och Stodkell reste stenen efter Vidbjörn, Krums gode arvinge. Vidbjörn högg.
