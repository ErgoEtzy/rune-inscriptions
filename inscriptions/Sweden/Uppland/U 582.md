## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Biorn ok "Igulfriðr ræistu stæin æftiR "Otrygg, sun sinn. Hann vaR drepinn a "Finnlandi.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Bjôrn ok "Ígulfríðr reistu stein eptir "Ótrygg, son sinn. Hann var drepinn á "Finnlandi.
```

## Translation to English

Bjôrn and Ígulfríðr raised the stone in memory of Ótryggr, their son. He was killed in Finland.

## Translation to Swedish

Björn och Igulfrid reste stenen efter Otrygg, sin son. Han blev dräpt i Finland.
