## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sigviðr ok "Ingvarr ok "Jarlabanki létu rista rúnar at "Ingvar, fôður sinn, ok at "Ragnvald, bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Sigviðr and Ingvarr and Jarlabanki had the runes carved in memory of Ingvarr, their father, and in memory of Ragnvaldr, their brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Sigvid och Ingvar och Jarlabanke lät rista runorna efter Ingvar, sin fader, och efter Ragnvald, sin broder.
