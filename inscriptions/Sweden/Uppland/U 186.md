## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Trani ok "Ásbjôrn þeir létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Jargeir, fôður sinn góðan.
```

## Translation to English

Trani and Ásbjôrn, they had this stone raised in memory of Jargeirr, their good father.

## Translation to Swedish

Trane och Åsbjörn de lät resa denna sten efter Jarger, sin gode fader.
