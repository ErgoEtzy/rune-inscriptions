## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ulfs arfar í "Lindey láta reisa þessa steina eptir fôður sinn ok bróður ok brú gerðu. "Véseti hjó.
```

## Translation to English

Ulfr's heirs í Lindey have raised these stones and made the bridge in memory of their father and brother. Véseti cut.

## Translation to Swedish

Ulvs arvingar i Lindö låter resa dessa stenar efter sin fader och broder och gjorde bron. Visäte högg.
