## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Fullugi lét hôggva stein ok þeir "Haukr/"Húkr báðir brœðr eptir "Manna/"Mána, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Fullugi had the stone cut, and (also) Haukr/Húkr, both brothers in memory of Manni/Máni, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Fulluge lät hugga stenen ochhan och Hök(?), båda bröderna efter Måne/Manne, sin fader.
