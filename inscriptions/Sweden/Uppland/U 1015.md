## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Iofurr let retta stæin æftiR sun sinn "Øring ok "Iofastu, konu sina.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jôfurr lét rétta stein eptir son sinn "Œring ok "Jófastu, konu sína.
```

## Translation to English

Jôfurr had the stone erected in memory of his son Œring, and Jófast, his wife.

## Translation to Swedish

Jovur lät resa stenen efter sin son Öring och Jofasta, sin hustru.
