## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þrotti ok "Asi ræisa stæin æftiR "AuðgæiR, b[roður] ... "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þrótti ok "Ási reisa stein eptir "Auðgeir, b[róður] ... "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Þrótti and Ási raise the stone in memory of Auðgeirr, (their) brother ... May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Trotte och Åse resa stenen efter Ödger, [sin] broder ... Gud hjälpe hans ande.
