## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þjalfi ok "Holmlaug létu reisa steina þessa alla at "Banka/"Bagga, son sinn. Er átti einn sér skip ok austr stýrði í "Ingvars lið. "Guð hjalpi ônd "Banka/"Bagga. "Áskell reist.
```

## Translation to English

Þjalfi and Holmlaug had all of these stones raised in memory of Banki/Baggi, their son, who alone owned a ship and steered to the east in Ingvarr's retinue. May God help Banki's/Baggi's spirit. Áskell carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Tjälve och Holmlög lät resa alla dessa stenar efter Banke/Bagge, sin son. Han ägde ensam ett skepp och styrde österut i Ingvars här. Gud hjälpe Bankes/Bagges ande. Äskil ristade.
