## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Bjôrn ok "Ásgeirr láta reisa stein eptir fôður sinn ok "Lokkr(?) eptir fôðurfôður "Órœkja.
```

## Translation to English

Bjôrn and Ásgeirr have raised the stone in memory of their father; and Lokkr(?) in memory of (her) father's father Órœkja.

## Translation to Swedish

Björn och Åsger låter resa stenen efter sin fader och Lock(?) efter [sin] farfader Orökja.
