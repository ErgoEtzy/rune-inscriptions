## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorfastr ok "Ketilvé þau létu stein rétta eptir "Þorstein, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Þorfastr and Ketilvé, they had the stone erected in memory of Þorsteinn, their father. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Torfast och Kättilvi, de lät resa stenen efter Torsten, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
