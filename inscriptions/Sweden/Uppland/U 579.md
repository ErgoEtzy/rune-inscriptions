## Translation to English

Viðbjôrn, Guðbjôrn's heir, erected the stone in memory of his brothers Ormi(?) and ... ; Geiri(?), Guði(?) (and) Ljúfr(?) in memory of their brothers.

## Translation to Swedish

Vidbjörn, Gudbjörns arvinge, reste stenen efter sina bröder Orme(?) och ... Gere(?), Gude(?), Ljuv(?) efter sina bröder.
