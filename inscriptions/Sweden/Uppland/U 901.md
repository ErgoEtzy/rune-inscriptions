## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
... "[K]arl ok "Ígulbjôrn létu rétta steina þessa ok gera brú þessa at "Jôfur, fôður si[nn] ... hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

... Karl and Ígulbjôrn had these stones erected and this bridge made in memory of Jôfurr, their father ... may help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

… Karl och Igulbjörn lät resa dessa stenar och göra denna bro efter Jovur, sin fader … hjälpe hans ande.

## Other information

En kristen begravningscen med gravvigning (benedictio sepulchri).
