## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sveinn risti rúnar þessar. "Ketill(?) lét hôggva ok "Manni/"Máni. Þeir létu hôggva eptir "Gunna, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Sveinn carved these runes. Ketill had (the stone) cut and Manni/Máni, they had (it) cut in memory of Gunni, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Sven ristade dessa runor. Kättil(?) lät hugga och Manne/Måne. De lät hugga efter Gunne, sin fader.
