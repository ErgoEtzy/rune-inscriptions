## Normalization to Old West Norse


## Translation to English

<tayr>, Knútr and Þorgísl and Eysteinn, they raised the stone in memory of Bjôrn, their father. Snari cut.

## Translation to Swedish

<tayr>, Knut och Torgisl och Östen de reste stenen efter Björn, sin fader. Snare högg.
