## Translation to English

<bel> had (the stone) cut in memory of Eysteinn, his father; Gillaug in memory of her husbandman. Líkbjôrn cut.

## Translation to Swedish

<bel> lät hugga efter Östen, sin fader, Gillög efter sin man. Likbjörn högg.
