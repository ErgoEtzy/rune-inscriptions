× þurkil · huk + ufhikr × huk × þusthin + þaiR ' ker(þ)u ' merki + þesa + at · uifast ' fa[þur · si](n)

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorkell ok "OfæigR ok "Þorstæinn þæiR gærðu mærki þessa at "Vifast, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorkell ok "Ófeigr ok "Þorsteinn þeir gerðu merki þessi at "Véfast, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þorkell and Ófeigr and Þorsteinn, they made these landmarks in memory of Véfastr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Torkel och Ofeg och Torsten, de gjorde dessa minnesmärken efter Vifast, sin fader.
