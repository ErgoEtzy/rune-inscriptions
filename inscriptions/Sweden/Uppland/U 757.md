## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Balli ok "Ingi[b]iorn(?) ok "Vigbiorn þæiR letu ræisa stæin at "Vibiorn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Balli ok "Ingi[b]jôrn(?) ok "Vígbjôrn þeir létu reisa stein at "Vébjôrn.
```

## Translation to English

Balli and Ingibjôrn(?) and Vígbjôrn, they had the stone raised in memory of Vébjôrn.

## Translation to Swedish

Balle och Ingebjörn och Vigbjörn, de lät resa stenen efter Vibjörn.
