## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Iobiorn ræisti stæina æftiR "Sygbiorn/"Søybiorn, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jóbjôrn reisti steina eptir "Sigbjôrn/"Seybjôrn, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Jóbjôrn raised the stones in memory of Sigbjôrn/Seybjôrn, his father.

## Translation to Swedish

Jobjörn reste stenarna efter Sigbjörn, sin fader.
