## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Áli/"Alli ok "Jôfurfast létu gera merki eptir "Jarl, fôður sinn, ok at "Gísl ok at "Ingimund. Hann var drepinn austr, sonr "Jarls. "Œpir risti.
```

## Translation to English

Áli/Alli and Jôfurfast had the landmark made in memory of Jarl, their father, and in memory of Gísl and in memory of Ingimundr. He, Jarl's son, was killed in the east. Œpir carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Åle/Alle och Jovurfast lät göra minnesmärket efter Jarl, sin fader, och efter Gisl och efter Ingemund. Han blev dräpt österut, Jarls son. Öpir ristade.
