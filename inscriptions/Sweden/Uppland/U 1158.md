## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Guðsteinn(?) ok "Eistr ok "... ok "Áki létu reisa stein [eptir] "Freygeir, fôður sinn. "Hlífsteinn hjó rúnar þessar.
```

## Translation to English

Guðsteinn(?) and Eistr and ... and Áki had the stone raised in memory of Freygeirr, their father. Hlífsteinn cut these runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Gudsten(?) och Est och … och Åke lät resa stenen (efter) Fröger, sin fader. Livsten högg dessa runor.
