## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ulfr hjó í "Báristôðum eptir "Ulf í "Skolhamri, mág sinn góðan. "Ulfkell lét hôggva.
```

## Translation to English

Ulfr of Báristaðir cut (the stone) in memory of Ulfr in Skolhamarr, his good kinsman-by-marriage. Ulfkell had (it) cut.

## Translation to Swedish

Ulv i Borresta högg efter Ulv i Skålhamra, sin gode frände. Ulvkell lät hugga.
