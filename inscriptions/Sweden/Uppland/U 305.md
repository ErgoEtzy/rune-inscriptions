## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian


## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Áskatla(?) lét hôggva stein eptir "Fulluga, ok "Illugi ok "Signý.
```

## Translation to English

Áskatla(?) had the stone cut in memory of Fullugi, and Illugi and Signy (also had it cut).

## Translation to Swedish

Åskatla lät hugga stenen efter Fulluge, och Illuge och Signy.
