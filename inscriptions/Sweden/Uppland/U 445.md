## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Kylfingr lét reisa stein at "Nesbjôrn, fôður si[nn], ok "Gillaug at bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Kylfingr had the stone raised in memory of Nesbjôrn, his father; and Gillaug in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Kylving lät resa stenen efter Näsbjörn, sin fader, och Gillög efter sin man.
