
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Eilafr ok "Ásmundr ok "Eysteinn létu reisa stein eptir "Ólaf, fôður sinn, en "Guðlaug at "Dan, son sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Eilafr and Ásmundr and Eysteinn had the stone raised in memory of Ólafr, their father; and Guðlaug in memory of Danr, her son.

## Translation to Swedish

Elov och Åsmund och Östen lät resa stenen efter Olov, sin fader, och Gudlög efter Dan, sin son.
