## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Kal/"Kali let haggva hælli þessa æftiR syni sina tva, ok þau "Ingiþora br[o] æina giærðu, mykit mærki fyriR margum(?) manni.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Kál/"Kali lét hôggva helli þessa eptir sonu sína tvá, ok þau "Ingiþóra br[ú] eina gerðu, mikit merki fyrir môrgum(?) manni.
```

## Translation to English

Kál/Kali had this rock-slab cut in memory of his two sons, and he and Ingiþóra made one bridge -- a great landmark for many(?) men.

## Translation to Swedish

Kål/Kale lät hugga denna häll efter sina två söner, och han och Ingetora gjorde en bro, ett stort minnesmärke inför många(?) män.
