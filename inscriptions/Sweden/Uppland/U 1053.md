## Transliteration

```text
[katl · ok iku]lef þaon ltu kea mrki iftR kul sun si[n (o)k · ketilmun]
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Kætill ok "Ingilæif þaun letu gæra mærki æftiR "Kul(?), sun sinn, ok "Kætilmund.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ketill ok "Ingileif þau létu gera merki eptir "Kol(?), son sinn, ok "Ketilmund.
```

## Translation to English

Ketill and Ingileif, they had the landmark made in memory of Kolr(?), their son, and Ketilmundr.

## Translation to Swedish

Kättil och Ingelev, de lät göra minnesmärket efter Kol(?), sin son, och Kättilmund.
