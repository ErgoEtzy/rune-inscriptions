## Translation to English

<i-rkiorþ> had the stone raised in memory of Ônundr, his father, and in memory of ... his mother. May God help their souls.

## Translation to Swedish

<i-rkiorþ> lät resa stenen efter Anund, sin fader, och efter ..., sin moder. Gud hjälpe deras själ.
