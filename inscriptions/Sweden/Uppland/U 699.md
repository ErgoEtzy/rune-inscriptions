## Translation to English

Ingileif had the stone raised in memory of Brúni, her husbandman. He died in christening robes in Denmark. Balli carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Ingelev lät resa stenen efter Brune, sin man. Han dog i Danmark i vita kläder. Balle ristade.

## Other information

Källström (2007a:386) menar att [-r]ist... kan läsas [n r]ist... och tolkas "ristade härpå(?)".
