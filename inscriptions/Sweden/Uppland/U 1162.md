## Normalization to Old West Norse


## Translation to English

Bjôrn and Halfdan had the stone raised in memory of Ôzurr, (their) father; and Þórunnr in memory of (her) husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Björn och Halvdan lät resa stenen efter Assur, (sin) fader, och Torunn (efter sin) man.
