## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sveinn ok "Bjôrn ok "Ígulfastr létu reisa stein eptir "Ígul, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Sveinn and Bjôrn and Ígulfastr had the stone raised in memory of Ígull, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Sven och Björn och Igulfast lät resa stenen efter Igul, sin fader.
