## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Andsvarr ok "Þorbjôrn létu hôggva stein eptir fôður sinn ok "Steinhildr [eptir] bónda sinn "Tata/"Tatta.
```

## Translation to English

Andsvarr and Þorbjôrn had the stone cut in memory of their father; and Steinhildr in memory of her husbandman Tati/Tatti.

## Translation to Swedish

Andsvar och Torbjörn lät hugga stenen efter sin fader och Stenhild (efter) sin man Tatte.
