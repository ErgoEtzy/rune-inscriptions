## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Aguti ok "Assurr ok "Biorn ræistu stæin þenna æftiR "Nokka, dræng goðan.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ágoti ok "Ôzurr ok "Bjôrn reistu stein þenna eptir "Nokka, dreng góðan.
```

## Translation to English

Ágoti and Ôzurr and Bjôrn raised this stone in memory of Nokki, a good valiant man.

## Translation to Swedish

Agute och Assur och Björn reste denna sten efter Nocke, en god ung man.
