## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ingifastr lét reisa stein þenna at "Þorkel, fôður sinn, ok at "Gunnhildi, móður sína. Þau druknuðu bæði.
```

## Translation to English

Ingifastr had this stone raised in memory of Þorkell, his father, and in memory of Gunnhildr, his mother. They both drowned.

## Translation to Swedish

Ingefast lät resa denna sten efter Torkel, sin fader, och efter Gunnhild, sin moder. De drunknade båda.
