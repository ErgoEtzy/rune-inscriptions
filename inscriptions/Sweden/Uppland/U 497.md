
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ragnfastr lét hôggva helli ok gera brú eptir "Ingifast, fôður sinn, ok "Ingifríði, móður sína.
```

## Translation to English

Ragnfastr had the rock-slab cut and the bridge made in memory of Ingifastr, his father, and Ingifríðr, his mother.

## Translation to Swedish

Ragnfast lät hugga hällen och göra bron efter Ingefast, sin fader, och Ingefrid, sin moder.
