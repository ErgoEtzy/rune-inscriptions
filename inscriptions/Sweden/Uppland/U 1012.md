## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Spraki ok "Þorbjôrn ok "Óþveginn ok "Ginnlaug ok "Áfríðr(?) þau létu rétta eptir "Bjôrn, fôðu[r] sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Spraki and Þorbjôrn and Óþveginn and Ginnlaug and Áfríðr(?), they had (this) erected in memory of Bjôrn, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Sprake och Torbjörn och Otvagen och Ginnlög och Åfrid(?) de lät uppresa (stenen) efter Björn, sin fader.
