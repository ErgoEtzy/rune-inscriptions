## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Holmsteinn ok "Hôsvi létu reisa stein eptir "Jóbjôrn, fôður sinn, ok "Gyríðr(?) eptir.
```

## Translation to English

Holmsteinn and Hôsvi had the stone raised in memory of Jóbjôrn, their father; and Gyríðr(?) in memory of (him).

## Translation to Swedish

Holmsten och Hosve lät resa stenen efter Jobjörn, sin fader, och Gyrid efter (honom).
