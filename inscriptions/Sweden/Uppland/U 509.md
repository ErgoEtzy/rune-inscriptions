## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Algautr let stæin haggva æftiR "ÞorgæiR, faður sinn, ok "Kætil, broður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Algautr lét stein hôggva eptir "Þorgeir, fôður sinn, ok "Ketil, bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Algautr had the stone cut in memory of Þorgeirr, his father, and Ketill, his brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Algöt lät hugga stenen efter Torger, sin fader, och Kättil, sin broder.
