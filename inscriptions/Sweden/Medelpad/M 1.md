## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Bergsveinn ok "Sigfastr ok "Friði reistu stein þenna eptir "Búri(?)/"Býri(?), fôður sinn. En "Farþegn markaði.
```

## Translation to English

Bergsveinn and Sigfastr and Friði raised this stone in memory of Búrir(?)/Býrir(?), their father. And Farþegn marked.

## Translation to Swedish

Bergsven/Barksven och Sigfast och Fride reste denna sten efter Byre(?), sin fader. Och Fartägn ristade.
