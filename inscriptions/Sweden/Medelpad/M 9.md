## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sigurðr ok "Þórir, "Heðinn ok "Hersir reistu stein þenna eptir "Þórð "Serðu(?), fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Sigurðr and Þórir (and) Heðinn and Hersir raised this stone in memory of Þórðr Serða(?), their father.

## Translation to Swedish

»Sigurd och Tore, Heden och Härse reste denna sten efter Tord Skända(?), sin fader.»
