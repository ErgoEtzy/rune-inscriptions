## Translation to English

<bariRþ> erected this stone in memory of Unn his brother. He was Arinbjôrn's son ... May God help Unn's spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

<bariRþ> uppreste denna sten till minne av Un, sin broder. Han var Arinbjörns son … Hjälpe(?) Gud(?) Uns ande.
