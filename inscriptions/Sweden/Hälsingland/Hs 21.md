## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ásmundr ok "Farþegn, þeir réttu stein þenna eptir "Þorketil, fôður sinn, á "Vatrungum. "Gunnborga fáði stein þenna, hin góða.
```

## Translation to English

Ásmundr and Farþegn, they erected this stone in memory of Þorketill of Vattrång, their father. Gunnborga the good coloured this stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Åsmund och Fartägn, de lät uppresa denna sten efter Torkättil, sin fader, i Vattrång. Gunnborga den goda ristade denna sten.
