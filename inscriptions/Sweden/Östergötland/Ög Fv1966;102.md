## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Gunnhvatr ræisþi stæin þannsi æftiR "Þorstæin ok "Þorlak, syni sina.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse


## Translation to English

Gunnhvatr raised this stone in memory of Þorsteinn and Þorlakr, his sons.

## Translation to Swedish

Gunnvat reste denna sten efter Torsten och Torlak, sina söner.
