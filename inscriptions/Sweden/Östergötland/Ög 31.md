```text
"Frøystæinn retti stæin þenna æftiR "Sigmund ok okætRi hann va byrð/broður.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Freysteinn rétti stein þenna eptir "Sigmund ok ókætri hann vá byrð/bróður.
```

## Translation to English

Freysteinn erected this stone in memory of Sigmundr, and less pleased (i.e. saddened) he bore the sorrow/he conveyed his brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Frösten reste denna sten efter Sigmund och mindre glad ditförde han bördan.

## Other information

Salberger (2003e) tolkar slutet som 'nedstämd/mindre glad bar han sorgen.'.
