## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Asa let giærva kumbl æftiR "Þorgisl, bonda sinn, ok "Þorgunn æftiR faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ása lét gera kuml eptir "Þorgísl, bónda sinn, ok "Þorgunnr eptir fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ása had the monument made in memory of Þorgísl, her husbandman, and Þorgunnr in memory of her father.

## Translation to Swedish

Åsa lät göra minnesmärket efter Torgisl, sin make, och Torgunn efter sin fader.
