## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Asa ok "ÞorgæiRR ok "Ali/"Alli [þau] letu ræisa æftiR "Tofa, eR varð haggvinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ása ok "Þorgeirr ok "Áli/"Alli [þau] létu reisa eptir "Tófa, er varð hôggvinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ása and Þorgeirr and Áli/Alli, they had (the stone) raised in memory of Tófi, who was cut down.

## Translation to Swedish

Åsa och Torger och Åle/Alle [de] lät resa (stenen) efter Tove, som blev ihjälslagen.
