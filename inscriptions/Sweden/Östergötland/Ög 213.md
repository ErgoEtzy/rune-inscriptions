## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Steinlaug lét leggja stein þenna yfir "Ôlvi, son sinn. "Guð hjalpi sál hans ok "Guðs móðir.
```

## Translation to English

Steinlaug had this stone laid over Ôlvir, her son. May God and God's mother help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Stenlög lät lägga denna sten över Alve/Ölve, sin son. Gud hjälpe hans själ och Guds moder.
