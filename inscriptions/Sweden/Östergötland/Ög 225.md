## Transliteration

```text
þruntr × risti + tsin × þinsa + aftiR · þurtsin [faþ](u)r sin
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þrondr ræisti stæin þennsa æftiR "Þorstæin, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þróndr reisti stein þenna eptir "Þorstein, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þróndr raised this stone in memory of Þorsteinn, his father.

## Translation to Swedish

Trond reste denna sten efter Torsten, sin fader.
