## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorstæinn ræisti stæin æftiR "Viseta, faður sinn, bonda nytan, eR vaR hann sunn "Gisa.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorsteinn reisti stein eptir "Véseta, fôður sinn, bónda nýtan, er var hann sonr "Gísa.
```

## Translation to English

Þorsteinn raised the stone in memory of Véseti, his father, a capable husbandman, who was Gísi's son.

## Translation to Swedish

Torsten reste stenen efter Visäte, sin fader, en duktig bonde, som var Gises son.
