## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sveinn/"Steinn/"Seinn ok "Þorbjôrn ok "Sandarr þeir réttu stein þenna eptir "Þorkel, bróður sinn, son "<si--m>.
```

## Translation to English

Sveinn/Steinn/Seinn and Þorbjôrn and Sandarr, they erected this stone in memory of Þorkell, his (ie Torbjôrn's) brother, son of <si--m>.

## Translation to Swedish

Sven/Sten/Sen och Torbjörn och Sandar, de reste denna sten efter Torkel, sin broder, son till <si--m>.

## Other information

Av sista ordet syntes 1991 endast s(i)--m (S. Lagman 1991). Ordet tolkas av Salberger (1990c:17ff) som 'Sigstens'.
