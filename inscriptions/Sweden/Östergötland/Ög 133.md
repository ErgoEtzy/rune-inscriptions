## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Broddr ræisþi stæin þennsa æftiR "Saxa, faður sinn, ok æftiR "Odd, broður sinn. "Guð hialpi þæim.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Broddr reisti stein þenna eptir "Saxa, fôður sinn, ok eptir "Odd, bróður sinn. "Guð hjalpi þeim.
```

## Translation to English

Broddr raised this stone in memory of Saxi, his father, and in memory of Oddr, his brother. May God help them.

## Translation to Swedish

Brodd reste denna sten efter Saxe, sin fader, och efter Udd, sin broder. Gud hjälpe dem.
