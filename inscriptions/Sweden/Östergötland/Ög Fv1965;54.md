## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"HaukR ræisþi æft hæfan dræng kumbl æft sun sinn "<ukiþila> dræng allgoðan. SaR het "Grimarr.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Haukr reisti ept hæfan dreng kuml ept son sinn "<ukiþila> dreng algóðan. Sá hét "Grímarr.
```

## Translation to English

Haukr raised the monument in memory of a competent valiant man, in memory of his son <ukiþila>, an excellent valiant man. He was called Grímarr.

## Translation to Swedish

Hök reste efter en duglig ung man minnesmärke efter sin son ..., en mycket god ung man. Han hette Grimar.

## Other information

Upptäcktes 1962, låg som grundsten i tornet. Salberger (i: ANF 1976:33ff) tolkar runföljden ukiþila som "ok Eðla".
