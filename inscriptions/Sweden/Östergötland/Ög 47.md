## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorfriða ræisti stæin þennsi æftiR "Æskel/"Isikil, faður sinn, ok "Asbiorn, broður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorfríða reisti stein þenna eptir "Áskel/"Ísjôkul, fôður sinn, ok "Ásbjôrn, bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þorfríða raised this stone in memory of Áskell/Ísjôkull, her father, and Ásbjôrn, her brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Torfrida reste denna sten efter Eskil/Isikel, sin fader, och Åsbjörn, sin broder.
