## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
§A "Geira lét gera kuml þessi yfir "Þórð, bónda sinn, ok "Sigríðr, mær, yfir sinn fôður. §B "Guð hjalpi sál hans.
```

## Translation to English

§A Geira had these monuments made over Þórðr, her husbandman, and Sigríðr the maiden over her father. §B May God help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Gera lät göra detta minnesmärke över Tord, sin man, och Sigrid, ungmön, över sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans själ.

## Other information
