## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gautr reisti eptir "Skera/"Skara, son sinn. "Ônundr hjó síðu.
```

## Translation to English

Gautr raised (the stone) in memory of Skeri/Skari, his son. Ônundr cut the side.

## Translation to Swedish

Göt reste (stenen) efter Skäre/Skare, sin son. Anund högg sidan.
