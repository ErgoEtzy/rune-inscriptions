## Translation to English

Dýri(?) placed this stone in memory of Ásgautr, his brother, who lived(?) in Jôtunstaðir(?) and Brúni, a very good valiant man.(?)

## Translation to Swedish

Dyre(?) satte denna sten efter Åsgöt, sin broder, som bodde(?) <iattin> <þikra> i Jättestad(?) och (efter) Brune, en mycket god ung man(?). (?)
