
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Hrœríkr reisti stein þenna eptir "Fróða ok "Ásbjôrn, sonu sína.
```

## Translation to English

Hrœríkr raised this stone in memory of Fróði and Ásbjôrn, his sons.

## Translation to Swedish

Rörik reste denna sten efter Frode och Åsbjörn, sina söner.
