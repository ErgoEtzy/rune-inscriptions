## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Halfdan reisti/reist stein þenna [eptir] "Hrólf, fôður sinn, var góðr.
```

## Translation to English

Halfdan raised/carved this stone in memory of Hrólfr, his father; (he) was good.

## Translation to Swedish

Halvdan reste/ristade denna sten (efter) Rolv, sin fader; (han) var god.
