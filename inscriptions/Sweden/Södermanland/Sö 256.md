## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Svarthôfði ok "Víðfari ok "Vébjôrn ok "Ônundr reistu stein þenna [at] "Freygeir(?), fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Svarthôfði and Víðfari and Vébjôrn and Ônundr raised this stone (in memory of) Freygeirr(?), their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Svarthövde och Vidfare och Vibjörn och Anund reste denna sten (efter) Fröger(?), sin fader.
