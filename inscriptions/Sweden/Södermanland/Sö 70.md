## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ketilhôfði ok "Steinketill þeir reistu stein þenna at "Sigketil, bróður sinn, snjallir synir "Ólôfar.
```

## Translation to English

Ketilhôfði and Steinketill, Ólôf's able sons, they raised this stone in memory of Sigketill, their brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Kättilhövde och Stenkättil de reste denna sten efter Sigkättil, sin broder, Olövs raska söner.
