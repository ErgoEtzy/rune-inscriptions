
## Translation to English

Auðbjôrn and Sigbjôrn and Guðbjôrn, those brothers had this stone cut in memory of Bjôrn, their father. Eysteinn.

## Translation to Swedish

Ödbjörn och Sigbjörn (och) Gudbjörn, de bröderna lät hugga denna sten efter Björn, sin fader. Östen.
