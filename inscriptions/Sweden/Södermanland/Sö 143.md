## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Øyarr let giæra kumbl baði æftiR "Ingulf, sun sinn. Hialpi "Krist and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Eyjarr lét gera kuml bæði eptir "Ingulf, son sinn. Hjalpi "Kristr ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Eyjarr had both of the monuments made in memory of Ingulfr, his son. May Christ help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Öjar lät göra båda minnesmärkena efter Ingulv, sin son. Hjälpe Krist hans ande.
