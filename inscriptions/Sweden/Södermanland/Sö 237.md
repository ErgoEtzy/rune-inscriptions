## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"ÞoriR ok "Þiagn letu ræisa stæin þenna æftiR "Fastbiorn, faður "Þiagns, ok "Anund, sun sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þórir ok "Þegn létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Fastbjôrn, fôður "Þegns, ok "Ônund, son sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þórir and Þegn had this stone raised in memory of Fastbjôrn, Þegn's father, and Ônundr, their son.

## Translation to Swedish

Tore och Tägn lät resa denna sten efter Fastbjörn, Tägns fader, och (Tore efter) Anund, sin son.
