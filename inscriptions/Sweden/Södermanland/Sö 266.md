## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Jórunnr lét reisa stein at "Andvétt, son sinn, ok at "Ônundar. "Ásbjôrn.
```

## Translation to English

Jórunnr had the stone raised in memory of Andvéttr, his son, and in memory of Ônundr's. Ásbjôrn.

## Translation to Swedish

Jorun lät resa stenen efter Andvätt, sin son, och efter Anunds. Åsbjörn.

## Other information

Källström (2007a:127) tar upp möjligheten att slutet kan tolkas "ok at AnundaR Asbiorn", dvs "och efter Anunds Åsbjörn".
