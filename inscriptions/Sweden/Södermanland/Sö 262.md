## Translation to English

Sveinn had the stone raised ... his good father and in memory of Guðvôr/Guðmarr/Guðvarr.

## Translation to Swedish

Sven lät resa stenen ... sin gode fader, och efter Gudvor/Gudmar/Gudvar.
