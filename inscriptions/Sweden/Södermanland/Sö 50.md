## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorbiorn ok "Frøylaug letu retta stæin æftiR "Øynd, sun sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorbjôrn ok "Freylaug létu rétta stein eptir "Eynd, son sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þorbjôrn and Freylaug had the stone erected in memory of Eyndr, their son.

## Translation to Swedish

Torbjörn och Frölög lät resa stenen efter Önd, sin son.
