## Translation to English

§A Nesbjôrn, Sparr's/Spôrr's son, raised the stone in memory of Sveinn, his brother, a þegn of strength. ... §B the stone in memory of Sveinn.

## Translation to Swedish

§A Näsbjörn, Spars son, reste stenen efter Sven, sin broder, en styrkans tägn ... §B stenen efter Sven.
