## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorðr ok "Toki ræistu stæin þannsi æftiR "Bysiu, faður sinn. Hann varð dauðr a knarri.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þórðr ok "Tóki reistu stein þenna eptir "Bysju, fôður sinn. Hann varð dauðr á knerri.
```

## Translation to English

Þórðr and Tóki raised this stone in memory of Bysja, their father. He died on the cargo-ship.

## Translation to Swedish

Tord och Toke reste denna sten efter Bysja, sin fader. Han blev död på skeppet (knarren).
