## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Eyvindr/"Eyindr lét reisa merki at "Bjôrn, en "Guð hjalpi ônd.
```

## Translation to English

Eyvindr/Eyindr had the landmark raised in memory of Bjôrn; and may God help (his) spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Övind lät resa minnesmärket efter Björn. Men Gud hjälpe anden.
