## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"GæiRhvatr ok "Anundr ok "OtamR [letu] retta stæin at "Byrstæin, broður sinn, saR vaR austr með "Ingvari, dræng sniallan, sun "LiføyaR.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Geirhvatr ok "Ônundr ok "Ótamr [létu] rétta stein at "Bjórstein, bróður sinn, sá var austr með "Ingvari, dreng snjallan, son "Lífeyjar.
```

## Translation to English

Geirhvatr and Ônundr and Ótamr had the stone erected in memory of Bjórsteinn, their brother. He was in the east with Ingvarr, an able valiant man, the son of Lífey.

## Translation to Swedish

Gervat och Anund och Otam (lät) resa stenen efter Byrsten, sin broder, som var österut med Ingvar, en rask ung man, Livös son.
