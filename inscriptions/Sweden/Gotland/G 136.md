## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
Þenna eptir "Heil[f]ús(?). Hann dó heima ok átti dóttur eina. Hon heitir "Heilvé. "Uddr "Valdinga gerði steina ok var(?) kostar ma[ðr]. "Danr ok "Bótbjôrn ristu.
```

## Translation to English

This (is placed) in memory of Heilfúss(?) He died at home and owned (= had) one daughter. She is called Heilvé. Uddr of Valdinga made the stones and he funded the monument. Dan and Bótbjôrn carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Denna efter Hailfos(?). Han dog hemma och hade en dotter. Hon heter Hailvi. Valdinga-Udd(?) gjorde stenarna och ... Dan och Botbjärn ristade.
