## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Sniolaug let ræisa stæin æftiR "Vilæif, bonda sinn, en "Øyniutr.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Snjólaug lét reisa stein eptir "Véleif, bónda sinn, en "Eynjótr.
```

## Translation to English

Snjólaug had the stone raised in memory of Véleifr, her husbandman. And Eynjótr (carved?).

## Translation to Swedish

Snölög lät resa stenen efter Vilev, sin man. Och Önjut (ristade).
