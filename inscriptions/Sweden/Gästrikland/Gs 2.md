## Transliteration

```text
[ily]iki : ok : f[uluiki × ok : þurkair ... ...- × sin × snilan] : kuþ ilubi on(t)[a]
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Illugi ok "Fullugi ok "ÞorgæiRR ... ... sinn sniallan. "Guð hialpi anda.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Illugi ok "Fullugi ok "Þorgeirr ... ... sinn snjallan. "Guð hjalpi anda.
```

## Translation to English

Illugi and Fullugi and Þorgeirr ... their able ... May God help (his) spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Illuge och Fulluge och Torger …, sin duglige ... Gud hjälpe anden.
