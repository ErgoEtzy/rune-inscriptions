## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Guðvarðr/"Guðvarr/"Guðvar let ræisa stæin þenna æftiR "Auða, sun sinn, ok "Karl æftiR "Stæin, sun sinn. "Guð hialpi salu.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Guðvarðr/"Guðvarr/"Guðvôr lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Auða, son sinn, ok "Karl eptir "Stein, son sinn. "Guð hjalpi sálu.
```

## Translation to English

Guðvarðr/Guðvarr/Guðvôr had this stone raised in memory of Auði, his/her son; and Karl in memory of Steinn, his son. May God help (their) souls.

## Translation to Swedish

Gudvard/Gudvar/Gudvor lät resa denna sten efter Öde, sin son, och Karl efter Sten, sin son. Gud hjälpe själen.
