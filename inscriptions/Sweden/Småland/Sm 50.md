## Transliteration

```text
: arinbiorn ÷ gørthe ÷ mi(k) ÷¶ uitku^nder ÷ prest^er ÷ skref : m(i)(k) :¶ ok ÷ hær ÷ skal : um ÷ st^u^nd ÷ stanta ÷
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Arinbiorn gærði mik, "Viðkunnr præstr skræif mik, ok her skal um stund standa.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Arinbjôrn gerði mik, "Víðkunnr prestr skrifaði mik, ok hér skal um stund standa.
```

## Translation to English

Arinbjôrn made me, Víðkunnr the priest wrote me, and here shall I stand for a while.

## Translation to Swedish

Arinbjörn gjorde mig, Vidkunn präst skrev mig, och här ska (jag) stå en tid.
