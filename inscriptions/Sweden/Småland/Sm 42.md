## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Tumi/"Tummi/"Dómi reisti stein þenna eptir "Ôzur, bróður sinn, þann er var skipari "Haralds konungs.
```

## Translation to English

Tumi/Tummi/Dómi raised this stone in memory of Ôzurr, his brother, he who was King Haraldr's seaman.

## Translation to Swedish

Tume/Tumme/Domme reste denna sten efter Assur, sin broder, som var skeppsman åt kung Harald.

## Other information
