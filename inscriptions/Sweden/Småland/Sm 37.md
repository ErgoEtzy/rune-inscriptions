## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ôzurr gerði kuml þessi eptir "Eynd/"Hvít, fôður sinn. Hann var manna mestr óníðingr, var yndr matar ok ómunr hatrs. Góðr þegn "Guðs trú góða hafði.
```

## Translation to English

Ôzurr made these monuments in memory of Eyndr/Hvítr, his father. He was the most unvillainous of men; was liberal with food and oblivious to hate. A good Þegn, (who) had good belief in God.

## Translation to Swedish

Assur gjorde dettas minnesmärke efter Öjvind/Vit/Vend, sin fader. Han var av människor den meste oniding, var frikostig med mat och inte sinnad för hat. Den gode tägnen hade god gudstro.
