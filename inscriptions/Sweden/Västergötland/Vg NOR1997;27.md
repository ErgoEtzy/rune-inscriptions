## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ulfr ok "Ôzurr reistu stein þenna eptir "Áslak, harða góðan þegn, fôður sinn, harða frýnan.
```

## Translation to English

Ulfr and Ôzurr raised this stone in memory of Áslakr, a very good Þegn, their father, very brave and valiant.

## Translation to Swedish

Ulv och Assur reste denna sten efter Åslak, en mycket god tägn, sin fader, (en) mycket vänsäll (man).
