## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þórir "Skorpa/"Skaufa reisti stein þenna eptir "Ketil, son sinn, ok eptir "Ólôf, fóstru sína.
```

## Translation to English

Þórir Rusk/Tuft(-bearer) raised this stone in memory of Ketill, his son, and in memory of Ólôf, his foster-mother/daughter

## Translation to Swedish

Tore skorpa/? reste denna sten efter sin son Kättil och efter Oluv, sin fosterdotter (fostermor).
