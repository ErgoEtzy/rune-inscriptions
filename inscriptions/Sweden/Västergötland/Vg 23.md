```text
þurþr × sati × stina × iftiR × nus(t)in × faþur sin
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorðr satti stæina æftiR "Øystæin, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þórðr setti steina eptir "Eystein, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þórðr placed the stones in memory of Eysteinn, his father.

## Translation to Swedish

Tord satte stenarna efter Östen, sin fader.
