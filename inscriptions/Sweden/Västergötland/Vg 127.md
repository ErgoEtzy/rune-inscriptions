## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ôlvir(?) reisti, ok "Áskatla, stein þenna eptir "Gunnar, son "Sigtryggs, dreng góðan. "Guð dróttinn bjargi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Ôlvir(?) and Áskatla raised this stone in memory of Gunnarr, Sigtryggr's son, a good valiant man. May Lord God save his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Ölver(?) och Åskatia reste denna sten efter Gunnar, Sigtryggs son, en god ung man. Herren Gud bärge hans ande.
