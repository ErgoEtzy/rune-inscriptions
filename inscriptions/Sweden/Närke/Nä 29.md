## Transliteration

```text
bofriþr : let resa eftir biurn : bruþur : si(n) : han uar farin fultrekila
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Bofriðr/"Botfriðr/"Botfreðr let ræisa æftiR "Biorn, broður sinn. Hann vaR farinn fulldrængila.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Bófríðr/"Bótfríðr/"Bótfreðr lét reisa eptir "Bjôrn, bróður sinn. Hann var farinn fulldrengila.
```

## Translation to English

Bófríðr/Bótfríðr/Bótfreðr had (the stone) raised in memory of Bjôrn, her/his brother. He had travelled valiantly.

## Translation to Swedish

Bofrid/Botfrid/Botfred lät resa (stenen) efter Björn, sin broder. Han hade manligen farit vida.
