## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Folkviðr ræisti stæina þasi alla at sun sinn "Heðin, broður "AnundaR. "Vræiðr hiogg runaR.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Folkviðr reisti steina þessa alla at son sinn "Heðin, bróður "Ônundar. "Vreiðr hjó rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Folkviðr raised all of these stones in memory of his son Heðinn, Ônundr's brother. Vreiðr cut the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Folkvid reste alla dessa stenar efter sin son Heden, Anunds broder. Vred högg runorna.
