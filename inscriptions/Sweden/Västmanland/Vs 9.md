## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Gisl let gærva bro æftiR "Asl/"Ôsl, sun sinn. Hann varð dauðr a "Ænglandi. "Guð hialpi hans and ok selu.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gísl lét gera brú eptir "Ásl/"Ôsl, son sinn. Hann varð dauðr á "Englandi. "Guð hjalpi hans ônd ok sálu.
```

## Translation to English

Gísl had the bridge made in memory of Ásl/Ôsl, his son. He died in England. May God help his spirit and soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Gisl lät göra bron efter Åsl/Osl, sin son. Han blev död i England. Gud hjälpe hans ande och själ.
