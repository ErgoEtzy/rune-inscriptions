## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Djúri ok "Jóhan létu reisa stein eptir "Vébjôrn, fôður [sinn]. "Litli risti rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Djúri and Jóhan had the stone raised in memory of Vébjôrn, their father. Litli carved the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Djure och Johan lät resa stenen efter Vibjörn, sin fader. Litle ristade runorna.
