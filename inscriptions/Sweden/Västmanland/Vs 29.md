## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Véseti ok "Halfdan létu hôggva stein eptir "Holma, fôður sinn, ok "Holmfast, bróður sinn. "Hlífsteinn risti rúnar þessar.
```

## Translation to English

Véseti and Halfdan had the stone cut in memory of Holmi, their father, and Holmfastr, their brother. Hlífsteinn carved these runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Visäte och Halvdan lät hugga stenen efter Holme, sin fader, och efter Holmfast, sin broder. Livsten ristade dessa runor.
