## Translation to English

Þorsteinn cut on the rock-slab in memory of Erinmundr, his son, and in memory of Geirbjôrn, his father.

## Translation to Swedish

Torsten högg på hällen efter Ärenmund, sin son, och efter Gerbjörn, sin fader.
