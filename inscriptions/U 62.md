## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ônundr lét reisa steina ... "... fôður sinn, ok "Ketilvé eptir bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ônundr had the stones raised ... his father; and Ketilvé in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Anund lät resa stenarna ... sin fader, och Kättilvi efter sin make.
