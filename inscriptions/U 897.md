## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Sigviðr, sunn "GillaugaR, ræisti runaR æftiR "Ragnælfi, sværu sina.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sigviðr, sonr "Gillaugar, reisti rúnar eptir "Ragnelfi, sværu sína.
```

## Translation to English

Sigviðr, Gillaug's son, raised the runes in memory of Ragnelfr, his mother-in-law.

## Translation to Swedish

Sigvid, Gillögs son, reste(!) runorna efter Ragnälv, sin svärmoder.
