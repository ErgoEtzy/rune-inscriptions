```text
"Sigstæinn let ræisa stæin þenn[a] æftiR "Ingvar, sun sinn. Hann varð austr dauðr.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sigsteinn lét reisa stein þenn[a] eptir "Ingvar, son sinn. Hann varð austr dauðr.
```

## Translation to English

Sigsteinn had this stone raised in memory of Ingvarr, his son. He died in the east.

## Translation to Swedish

Sigsten lät resa denna sten efter Ingvar, sin son. Han blev död österut.
