## Translation to English

Ásmundr and <e-iR>, Gyríðr's sons, placed the stone in memory of Sigulfr, their father, Sóti's brother. Ulfkell, of Lófi's retinue, cut the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Åsmund och <e-iR>, Gyrids söner, satte stenen efter Sigulv, sin fader, Sotes broder. Ulvkel högg runorna, Loves följesman.
