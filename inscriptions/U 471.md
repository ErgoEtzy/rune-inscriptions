## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Fullugi ok "Ulfr ok "Ágeirr(?) létu reisa stein eptir "Ônund, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Fullugi and Ulfr and Ágeirr(?) had the stone raised in memory of Ônundr, their father. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Fulluge och Ulv och Åger(?) lät resa stenen efter Anund, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
