## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorkell ok "Fullugi letu haggva hælli þessa ok bro gærva æftiR "Stæin, faður senn. "OlæifR hiogg.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorkell ok "Fullugi létu hôggva helli þessa ok brú gera eptir "Stein, fôður sinn. "Óleifr hjó.
```

## Translation to English

Þorkell and Fullugi had this rock-slab cut and the bridge made in memory of Steinn, their father. Óleifr cut.

## Translation to Swedish

Torkel och Fulluge lät hugga denna häll och göra bron efter Sten, sin fader. Olev högg.
