## Translation to English

Loðinn had the stone raised ... his father ... his(?) ... May God help his spirit. Þorbjôrn cut.

## Translation to Swedish

Luden lät resa stenen …, sin fader … sin … Gud hjälpe hans ande. Torbjörn högg.
