## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian


## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Slóði, "Ragnfríðr, þau létu hôggva stein eptir "Ígulbjôrn, son sinn. Hjalpi "Kristr ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Slóði (and) Ragnfríðr, they had the stone cut in memory of Ígulbjôrn, their son. May Christ help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Slode, Ragnfrid de lät hugga stenen efter Igulbjörn, sin son. Hjälpe Krist hans ande.
