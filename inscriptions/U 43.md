## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
§A "Ófeigr ok "Sigmarr ok "Freybjôrn þeir reistu at "Jôrund, fôður sinn, bónda "Gunnu. §B "Ernfastr hjó rúnar þessar.
```

## Translation to English

§A Ófeigr and Sigmarr and Freybjôrn, they raised (the stone) in memory of Jôrundr, their father, Gunna's husbandman. §B Ernfastr cut these runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Ofeg och Sigmar och Fröbjörn de reste (stenen) efter Jörund, sin fader, Gunnas make. Ärnfast högg dessa runor.
