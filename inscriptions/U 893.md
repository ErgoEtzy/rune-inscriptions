## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Eyndr ok "Ônundr ok "Ingigerðr létu reisa stein at "Víg[b]jôrn, fôður sinn. "Œpir risti.
```

## Translation to English

Eyndr and Ônundr and Ingigerðr had the stone raised in memory of Vígbjôrn, their father. Œpir carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Önd och Anund och Ingegärd lät resa stenen efter Vigbjörn, sin fader. Öpir ristade.
