+ kitil×fastr × risti × stin + þina × iftiR × askut × faþur + sin × saR × uas × uistr × uk × ustr + kuþ ialbi × as × salu

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Kætilfastr ræisti stæin þenna æftiR "Asgaut, faður sinn. SaR vas vestr ok austr. "Guð hialpi hans salu.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ketilfastr reisti stein þenna eptir "Ásgaut, fôður sinn. Sá var vestr ok austr. "Guð hjalpi hans sálu.
```

## Translation to English

Ketilfastr raised this stone in memory of Ásgautr, his father. He was in the west and in the east. May God help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Kättilfast reste denna sten efter Åsgöt, sin fader. Denne var västerut och österut. Gud hjälpe hans själ.

## Other information

Oornamenterad, endast enkelt kors.
