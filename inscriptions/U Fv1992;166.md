## Translation to English

§A Mari(?) gave the rib-bone. (He) owns the most wealth. ... Ása(?) §BP The king is the best man(?). He owns the most wealth. He is agreeable. §BQ A king is the best man. He owns the most wealth. He is ever ruler.
