## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Bjôrn reisti stein þenna eptir "Þjóð, félaga sinn. "Guð hjalpi ... sálu ok sú helga "Sankta "María.
```

## Translation to English

Bjôrn raised this stone in memory of Þjóðr, his partner. May God and the holy Saint Mary help ... soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Björn reste denna sten efter Tjod, sin bolagsman. Gud hjälpe ... själ och den heliga sankta Maria.
