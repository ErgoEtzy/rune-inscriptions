## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ulfr ok "Herbjôrn ok "Nesbjôrn ok "Hemingr létu reisa stein at "Borgulf, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ulfr and Herbjôrn and Nesbjôrn and Hemingr had the stone raised in memory of Borgulf, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Ulv och Härbjörn och Näsbjörn och Häming lät resa stenen efter Borgulv, sin fader.
