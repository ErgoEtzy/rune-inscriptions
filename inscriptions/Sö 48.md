## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"ViglæikR ok "Kiuli/"Gylli ok "Hælgi ok "Igulfastr, þæiR ræistu stæin æftiR "Þorbiorn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Vígleikr ok "Kjúli/"Gylli ok "Helgi ok "Ígulfastr, þeir reistu stein eptir "Þorbjôrn.
```

## Translation to English

Vígleikr and Kjúli/Gylli and Helgi and Ígulfastr, they raised the stone in memory of Þorbjôrn.

## Translation to Swedish

Viglek och Kjule/Gylle och Helge och Holmfast de reste stenen efter Torbjörn.
