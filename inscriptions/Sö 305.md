## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sibbi ok "Tjôrvi létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Þorkel, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Sibbi and Tjôrvi had this stone raised in memory of Þorkell, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Sibbe och Tjarve lät resa denna sten efter Torkel, sin fader.
