## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Freysteinn ok "Káti í "Kœlingi þeir létu reisa stein þenna, ok "Bersa/"Birsa, eptir "Kvígbjôrn, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Freysteinn and Káti of Kœlingi and Bersa/Birsa, they had this stone raised in memory of Kvígbjôrn, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Frösten och Kåte i Kölinge de lät resa denna sten, och Bärsa/Birsa, efter Kvigbjörn, sin fader.
