## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gerðarr ok "Jôrundr láta reisa þessa steina eptir systursonu sína "Ernmund ok "Ingimund.
```

## Translation to English

Gerðarr and Jôrundr have raised these stones in memory of their sister's sons Ernmundr and Ingimundr.

## Translation to Swedish

Gärdar och Jorund låter resa dessa stenar efter sina systersöner Ärnmund och Ingemund.
