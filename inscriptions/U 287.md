## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"GiulakR(?)/"KiulakR(?) let ræisa stæin æftiR sun sinn "Ingifast, ok "Ingvarr ok(?) at broður sinn, en "ØpiR risti runaR.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Kjallakr(?)/"Kjullakr(?) lét reisa stein eptir son sinn "Ingifast, ok "Ingvarr ok(?) at bróður sinn, en "Œpir risti rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Kjallakr(?)/Kjullakr(?) had the stone raised in memory of his son Ingifastr; and Ingvarr also(?) in memory of his brother. And Œpir carved the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Gjulak(?)/Kjulak(?) lät resa stenen efter sin son Ingefast, och Ingvar (lät) också (resa stenen) efter sin broder. Och Öpir ristade runorna.
