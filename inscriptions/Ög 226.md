## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian


## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þóra ok "Áslakr þeir létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Ingimar, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þóra and Áslakr, they had this stone raised in memory of Ingimarr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Tora och Åslak de lät resa denna sten efter Ingemar, sin fader.

## Other information

Ög 227 och Ög 226 avser samma runsten.
