## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Tora let ræisa stæin þenna æftiR "Rolæif, sun sinn. "Guð hialpi and hans. "Fotr risti.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Tóra lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Hróðleif, son sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans. "Fótr risti.
```

## Translation to English

Tóra had this stone raised in memory of Hróðleifr, her son. May God help his spirit. Fótr carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Tora lät resa denna sten efter Rolev, sin son. Gud hjälpe hans ande. Fot ristade.
