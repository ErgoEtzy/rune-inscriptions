## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Signjótr ok "Áviðr létu reisa stein at "Ófeig, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans ok "Guðs móðir. "Fótr risti rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Signjótr and Áviðr had the stone raised in memory of Ófeigr, their father. May God and God's mother help his spirit. Fótr carved the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Signjut och Åvid lät resa stenen efter Ofeg, sin fader. Gud och Guds moder hjälpe hans ande. Fot ristade runorna.
