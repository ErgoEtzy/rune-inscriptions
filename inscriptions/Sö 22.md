## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þr[jó]zkr ok "Frosti létu gera brú ok létu reisa stein at "Orma, fôður sinn góðan.
```

## Translation to English

Þrjózkr and Frosti had the bridge made and had the stone raised in memory of Ormi, their good father.

## Translation to Swedish

Tredsk och Froste lät göra bron och lät resa stenen efter Orme, sin gode fader.
