## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Rúnviðr lét reisa stein eptir "Svein, fôður sinn ... <kls-i> "Jôfurr ok "Fox létu reisa stein eptir fôður sinn "Kára.
```

## Translation to English

Rúnviðr had the stone raised in memory of Sveinn, his father ... Jôfurr and Foks had the stone raised in memory of their father Kári.

## Translation to Swedish

Runvid lät resa stenen efter Sven, sin fader ... Jovur och Fuks lät resa stenen efter sin fader Kåre.
