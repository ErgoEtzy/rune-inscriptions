## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Andvéttr ok "Gerðarr létu rista rúnar þessar eptir "Svein, fôður sinn, ok "Ketilvé eptir bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Andvéttr and Gerðarr had these runes carved in memory of Sveinn, their father; and Ketilvé in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Andvätt och Gärdar lät rista dessa runor efter Sven, sin fader, och Kättilvi efter sin make.
