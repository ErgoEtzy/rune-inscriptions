## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gísl ok "Ingimundr, góðir drengir, gera létu merki at "Halfdan, fôður sinn, ok at "Eydísi, móður sína. "Guð hjalpi sál hennar vel nú.
```

## Translation to English

Gísl and Ingimundr, good valiant men, had the landmark made in memory of Halfdan, their father and in memory of Eydís, their mother. May God now help her soul well.

## Translation to Swedish

Gisl och Ingemund, goda unga män, lät göra minnesmärket efter Halvdan, sin fader, och efter Ödis, sin moder. Gud hjälpe nu väl hennes själ.
