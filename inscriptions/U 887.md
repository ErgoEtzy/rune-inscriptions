## Translation to English

Jógeirr and Fastgeirr and Auðríkr/Eyríkr had the stone erected in memory of Borggeirr, their acclaimed father. Auðríkr/Eyríkr cut these runes himself in memory of his father. Interpret, he who can.

## Translation to Swedish

Joger och Fastger och Ödrik/Örik lät uppresa stenen efter Borgger, sin frejdade fader. Själv högg Ödrik/Örik efter sin fader dessa runor. Tyde den som kan.

## Other information

Enda kända stenen av Örik, ornamentiken mycket skickligt gjord.
