## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Austmaðr, "Guðfastar sonr, lét reis[a stei]n þenna ok gera brú þessa ok hann lét kristna "Jamtaland. "Ásbjôrn gerði brú, "Trjónn reist ok "Steinn rúnar þessar.
```

## Translation to English

Austmaðr, Guðfastr's son had this stone raised and this bridge made and he had Jamtaland Christianized. Ásbjôrn made the bridge, Trjónn and Steinn carved these runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Östman, Gudfasts son, lät resa denna sten och göra denna bro, och han lät kristna Jämtland. Åsbjörn gjorde bron. Tryn och Sten ristade dessa runor.
