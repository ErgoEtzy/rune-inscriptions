## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gamall ok "Þjalfi þeir létu reisa stein þenna ... fôður sinn, mann nýtan. Hjalpi "Guð sál hans.
```

## Translation to English

Gamall and Þjalfi, they had this stone raised ... their father, a capable man. May God help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Gammal och Tjälve de lät resa denna sten ... sin fader, en dugande man. Gud hjälpe hans själ.
