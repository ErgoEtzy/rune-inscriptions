## Transliteration

```text
+ halftan : let : reisa : stein : þen(s)a : eftir : iukeir : broþur : sin : nuk : kufriþ : eftiR : sun : sin : (k)uþ : hialbi : at : hans
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Halfdan let ræisa stæin þennsa æftiR "IogæiR, broður sinn, ok "Guðfriðr æftiR sun sinn. "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Halfdan lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Jógeir, bróður sinn, ok "Guðfríðr eptir son sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Halfdan had this stone raised in memory of Jógeirr, his brother; and Guðfríðr in memory of her son. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Halvdan lät resa denna sten efter Joger, sin broder, och Gudfrid efter sin son. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
