## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"OlæifR ok "ÞoriR ok "Tofa ræistu stæin þenna æftiR "Guðmund, goðan bonda.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Óleifr ok "Þórir ok "Tófa reistu stein þenna eptir "Guðmund, góðan bónda.
```

## Translation to English

Óleifr and Þórir and Tófa raised this stone in memory of Guðmundr, the good husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Olev och Tore och Tova reste stenarna efter Gudmund, en god bonde.
