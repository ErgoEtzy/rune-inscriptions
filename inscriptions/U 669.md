
## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Styrkárr ok "Hjôrvarðr létu reisa þenna stein eptir "Gísl, bróður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ôndinni. "Véseti ok "Ófeigr þeir hjoggu.
```

## Translation to English

Styrkárr and Hjôrvarðr had this stone raised in memory of Gísl, their brother. May God help (his) spirit. Véseti and Ófeigr, they cut.

## Translation to Swedish

Styrkar och Hjorvard lät resa denna sten efter Gisl, sin broder. Gud hjälpe anden. Visäte och Ofeg de högg.

## Other information

Parsten till U 668, var inmurad i samma källare som denna.
