## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
DrængiaR ræisþu stæin þennsi æft "Græip, gilda sinn, "Lofi ræist runaR þessaR, "Iuta sun.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
Drengir reistu stein þenna ept "Greip, gilda sinn, "Lófi reist rúnar þessar, "Júta son.
```

## Translation to English

Valiant men raised this stone in memory of Greipr, their guild-brother - Lófi carved these runes - Júti's son.

## Translation to Swedish

Unga män reste denna sten efter Grep, sin gillesbroder - Love ristade runorna - Jutes son.

## Other information

Salberger (1990c:26ff) tolkar slutet som 'Jutes duktige son'.
