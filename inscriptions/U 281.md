## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Vibiorn ok "Huskarl letu r[æis]a stæin æftiR "Sibba, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Vébjôrn ok "Húskarl létu r[eis]a stein eptir "Sibba, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Vébjôrn and Húskarði had the stone raised in memory of Sibbi, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Vibjörn och Huskarl lät resa stenen efter Sibbe, sin fader.
