## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorgísl ok "Þorsteinn ok "Vébjôrn ok "Óleifr [lé]tu reisa stein eptir "Þorbjôrn, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þorgísl and Þorsteinn and Vébjôrn and Óleifr had the stone raised in memory of Þorbjôrn, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Torgisl och Torsten och Vibjörn och Olev lät resa stenen efter Torbjörn, sin fader.
