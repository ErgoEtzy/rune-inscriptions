## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text

```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Goti lét reisa ste[i]n þenna eptir "Ingjald, bróður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Goti had this stone raised in memory of Ingjaldr, his brother. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Gute lät resa denna sten efter Ingjald, sin broder. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
