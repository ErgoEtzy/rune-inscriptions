## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ketilfastr, "Nefr(?), "Ófeigr "<--iutr> þeir létu stein eptir "Ingjald, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ketilfastr, Nefr(?), Ófeigr ... they had the stone (raised) in memory of Ingjaldr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Kättilfast, Näv(?), Ofeg, <--iutr>, de lät (resa) stenen efter Ingjald, sin fader.
