## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Illugi ok "Fullugi þeir létu reisa stein þenna eptir fôður sinn "Arngísl. "Guð hjalpi anda hans. "Þorfastr risti rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Illugi and Fullugi, they had this stone raised in memory of their father Arngísl. May God help his spirit. Þorfastr carved the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Illuge och Fulluge de lät resa denna sten efter sin fader Arngisl. Gud hjälpe hans ande. Torfast ristade runorna.

## Other information

En fågel mitt på och två människofigurer längst ned fasthållna av rundjurens klor.
