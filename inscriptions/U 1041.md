## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Hagbarðr ok "Ingigerðr létu rétta steina eptir fôður sinn "Rúnfast ok eptir "Rúnu, móður sína. "Ingulfr risti rúnar. "Hagbarðr gerði brú.
```

## Translation to English

Hagbarðr and Ingigerðr had the stones erected in memory of their father Rúnfastr and in memory of Rúna, their mother. Ingulfr carved the runes. Hagbarðr made the bridge.

## Translation to Swedish

Hagbard och Ingegärd lät uppresa stenarna efter sin fader Runfast och efter Runa, sin moder. Ingulv ristade runorna. Hagbard gjorde bron.
