## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Finnviðr ræisti stæin þenna æftiR broður sinn, "Þorðr, "Þialfa sun. "Guð hialpi and hans ok "Guðs moðiR. Hann gærði bro at broður sinn, ok "Asa, moðiR þæiRa.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Finnviðr reisti stein þenna eptir bróður sinn, "Þórðr, "Þjalfa son. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans ok "Guðs móðir. Hann gerði brú at bróður sinn, ok "Ása, móðir þeira.
```

## Translation to English

Finnviðr raised this stone in memory of his brother Þórðr, Þjalfi's son. May God and God's mother help his spirit. He made the bridge in memory of his brother and (so did) Ása, their mother.

## Translation to Swedish

Finnvid reste denna sten efter sin broder Tord, Tjälves son. Gud hjälpe hans ande och Guds moder. Han gjorde bron efter sin broder, och Åsa, deras moder (lät också göra).
