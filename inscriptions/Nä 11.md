## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian


## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Bjôrn ok "Geirhvatr létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Eibjôrn, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi sálu hans.
```

## Translation to English

Bjôrn and Geirhvatr had this stone raised in memory of Eibjôrn, their father. May God help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Björn och Gervat lät resa denna sten efter Ebjörn, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans själ.
