```text
"Aguti let retta st[æi]n þenna æftiR "Tuma/"Tumma ... "[K]ristr lati koma and "Tuma/"Tumma i lius ok paradisi ok i þann hæim bæzta kristnum.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ágoti lét rétta st[ei]n þenna eptir "Tuma/"Tumma ... "[K]ristr láti koma ônd "Tuma/"Tumma í ljós ok paradísi ok í þann heim bezta kristnum.
```

## Translation to English

Ágoti had this stone erected in memory of Tumi/Tummi ... May Christ let Tumi's/Tummi's spirit come into light and paradise and into the best home for Christians.

## Translation to Swedish

Agute lät resa denna sten efter Tume/Tumme ... Krist  låte Tumes/Tummes själ komma i ljus och paradis och i den bästa värld för kristna.
