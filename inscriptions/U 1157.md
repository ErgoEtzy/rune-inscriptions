```text
þurstain + raisti + stain + þina + aftir + halftan × kuþ halbi + ant × hans
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorstæinn ræisti stæin þenna æftiR "Halfdan. "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorsteinn reisti stein þenna eptir "Halfdan. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Þorsteinn raised this stone in memory of Halfdan. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Torsten reste denna sten efter Halvdan. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
