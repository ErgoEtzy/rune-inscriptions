## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Skalli ok "Sibbi ok "Lifstæinn letu ræisa stæin æftiR "Andvett(?), faður sinn, en "Gillaug at bonda sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Skalli ok "Sibbi ok "Hlífsteinn létu reisa stein eptir "Andvétt(?), fôður sinn, en "Gillaug at bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Skalli and Sibbi and Hlífsteinn had the stone raised in memory of Andvéttr(?), their father; and Gillaug in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Skalle och Sibbe och Livsten lät resa stenen efter Andvätt, sin fader, och Gillög efter sin make.
