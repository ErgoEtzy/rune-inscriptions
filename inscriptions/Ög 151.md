## Translation to English

Þorbjôrn(?)/Dýrbjôrn(?) raised/erected this stone in memory of <uisþi>, Sveinn's brother.

## Translation to Swedish

Torbjörn(?)/Dyrbjörn(?) reste denna sten efter <uisþi>, Svens(?) broder.
