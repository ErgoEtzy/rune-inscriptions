## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorbjôrn ok "<kurþi> ok "Víðfari, þeir létu reisa merki þessi at "Vébjôrn, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þorbjôrn and <kurþi> and Víðfari, they had these landmarks raised in memory of Vébjôrn, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Torbjörn och <kurþi> och Vidfare, de lät resa dessa minnesmärken efter Vibjörn, sin fader.
