## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sigdjarfr ok "Holmbjôrn ok "Þorbjôrn þeir létu rétta stein þenna ok brú gera at "Jón, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Sigdjarfr and Holmbjôrn and Þorbjôrn, they had this stone erected and the bridge made in memory of Jón, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Sigdjärv och Holmbjörn och Torbjörn de lät uppresa denna sten och göra bron efter Jon, sin fader.
