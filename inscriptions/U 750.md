## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Sæbiorn ok "FrøygæiRR þæiR letu ræisa stæin þennsa at "Holmfast/"Hialmfast, broður sinn. "Balli risti.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sæbjôrn ok "Freygeirr þeir létu reisa stein þenna at "Holmfast/"Hjalmfast, bróður sinn. "Balli risti.
```

## Translation to English

Sæbjôrn and Freygeirr, they had this stone raised in memory of Holmfastr/Hjalmfastr, their brother. Balli carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Säbjörn och Fröger, de lät resa denna sten efter Holmfast, sin broder. Balle ristade.
