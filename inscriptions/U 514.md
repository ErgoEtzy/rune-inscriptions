## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Anundr let ok gærva bro ok stæin retti æftiR "Kætilfast, faður sinn, ok "FarulfR.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ônundr lét ok gera brú ok stein rétti eptir "Ketilfast, fôður sinn, ok "Farulfr.
```

## Translation to English

Ônundr also had the bridge made and the stone erected in memory of Ketilfastr, his father, and Farulfr (also made).

## Translation to Swedish

Anund lät också göra bron och reste stenen efter Kättilfast, sin fader, och Farulv.
