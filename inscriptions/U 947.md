## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Þorstæinn ok "Vigi letu gæra bro þessi fyriR "AssuraR and, mags sins. Nu es sal sagat sva: hialpi "Guð. "Æsbiorn gærði(?).
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þorsteinn ok "Vígi létu gera brú þessa fyrir "Ôzurar ônd, mágs síns. Nú er sál sagt svá: hjalpi "Guð. "Ásbjôrn gerði(?).
```

## Translation to English

Þorsteinn and Vígi had this bridge made for Ôzurr's spirit, their kinsman-by-marriage. This is now said for his soul: may God help. Ásbjôrn made.(?)

## Translation to Swedish

Torsten och Vige lät göra denna bro för Assurs, sin svågers, ande. Nu är så sagt för själen: hjälpe Gud. Åsbjörn gjorde(?).
