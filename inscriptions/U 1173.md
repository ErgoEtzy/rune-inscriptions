## Transliteration

```text
' ari + rasti + stain + aftir + (h)ialm + faþur sin + kuþ + hialbi + ant hans
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Ari ræisti stæin æftiR "Hialm, faður sinn. "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ari reisti stein eptir "Hjalm, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Ari raised the stone in memory of Hjalmr, his father. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Are reste stenen efter Hjälm, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans ande.

## Other information
