## Transliteration

```text
biurn × auk × uikuntr × litu raisa × stin × eftiR × suin × fostrsun × sin
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Biorn ok "Vigunnr letu ræisa stæin æftiR "Svæin, fostrsun sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Bjôrn ok "Végunnr létu reisa stein eptir "Svein, fóstrson sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Bjôrn and Végunnr had the stone raised in memory of Sveinn, their foster-son.

## Translation to Swedish

Björn och Vigunn lät resa stenen efter Sven, sin fosterson.
