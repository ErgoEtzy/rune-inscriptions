## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ámundi ok "Auðgerðr létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Ônund, son sinn, ok brú gera.
```

## Translation to English

Ámundi and Auðgerðr had this stone raised and the bridge made in memory of Ônundr, their son.

## Translation to Swedish

Amunde och Ödgärd lät resa denna sten efter Anund, sin son, och göra bro.
