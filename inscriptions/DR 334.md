## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Faðir lét hôggva rúnar þessar eptir "Ôzur, bróður sinn, er norðr varð dauðr í víkingu.
```

## Translation to English

Faðir had these runes cut in memory of Ôzurr, his brother, who died in the north on a viking raid.

## Translation to Swedish

Fader lät hugga dessa runor efter Assur, sin broder, som norrut blev död i viking.
