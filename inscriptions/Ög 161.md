## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sveinn ok þeir brœðr reistu stein eptir fôður sinn "Jarl. "Guð h[ja]lpi ônd hans ok "Guðs móð[i]r í ljós.
```

## Translation to English

Sveinn and his brothers raised the stone in memory of their father Jarl. May God and God's mother help his spirit into light.

## Translation to Swedish

Sven och hans bröder reste stenen efter sin fader Jarl. Gud hjälpe hans ande och Guds moder till ljuset.
