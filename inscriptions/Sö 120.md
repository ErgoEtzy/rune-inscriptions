## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Fastulfr, sonr "Véulfs, lét hôggva stein at "Holmgeir, bróður sinn, ok "Þorvôr, móður sína. "Guð hjalpi ônd þeira.
```

## Translation to English

Fastulfr, Véulfr's son, had the stone cut in memory of Holmgeirr, his brother and Þorvôr, his mother. May God help their spirits.

## Translation to Swedish

Fastulv, Viulvs son, lät hugga stenen efter Holmger, sin broder, och Torvar, sin moder. Gud hjälpe deras ande.

## Other information
