```text
uibiarn lit · hkua stain · iftiR · kuna totur · kunu sina
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Vibiorn let haggva stæin æftiR "Gunna dottur, kunu sina.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Vébjôrn lét hôggva stein eptir "Gunna dóttur, konu sína.
```

## Translation to English

Vébjôrn had the stone cut in memory of Gunni's daughter, his wife.

## Translation to Swedish

Vibjörn lät hugga stenen efter Gunnes dotter, sin hustru.
