## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Geirvé ok "Gulla reistu stein þenna eptir "Ônund, fôður sinn. Er var austr dauðr með "Ingvari. "Guð hjalpi ônd "Ônundar.
```

## Translation to English

Geirvé and Gulla raised this stone in memory of Ônundr, their father, who died in the east with Ingvarr. May God help Ônundr's spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Gervi och Gulla reste denna sten efter Anund, sin fader. Han blev död österut med Ingvar. Gud hjälpe Anunds ande.
