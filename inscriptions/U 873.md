## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þingfastr lét reisa stein þenna at "Holmgeir, fôður sinn góðan, "Guð hjalpi sál hans, bónda "Ingu. "Balli risti stein þenna.
```

## Translation to English

Þingfastr had this stone raised in memory of Holmgeirr, his good father, Inga's husbandman. May God help his soul. Balli carved this stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Tingfast lät resa denna sten efter Holmger, sin gode fader, Ingas man. Gud hjälpe hans själ. Balle ristade denna sten.
