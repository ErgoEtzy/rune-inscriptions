## Normalization to Old West Norse

## Translation to English

Fíli, Svarthôfði, Djarfr (and) Ófeigr raised the stone in memory of Ósyrg, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

File, Svarthövde, Djärv, Ofeg reste stenen efter Osyrg, sin fader.
