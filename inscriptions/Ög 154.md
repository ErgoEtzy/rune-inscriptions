## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ása lét gera kuml þetta eptir "Eyjar, fôður sinn góðan. "Guð hjalpi sálu "Eyjars.
```

## Translation to English

Ása had this monument made in memory of Eyjarr, her good father. May God help Eyjarr's soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Åsa lät göra detta minnesmärke efter Öjar, sin gode fader. Gud hjälpe Öjars själ.
