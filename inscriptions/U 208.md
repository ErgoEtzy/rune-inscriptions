## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
Ristu/Reistu merki at mann mætan, synir algóðir at sinn fôður "Styrkár. "Véseti risti steina.
```

## Translation to English

In memory of their father Styrkárr, excellent sons carved/raised the landmark in memory of a venerable man. Véseti carved the stones.

## Translation to Swedish

Minnesmärke ristade/reste efter en ansedd man, fullgoda söner efter sin fader Styrkår. Visäte ristade stenarna.
