## Translation to English

Gulleifr and Sjóli had (the stone) raised in memory of Ingifastr, Jôrundr's/Jôrundi's son.

## Translation to Swedish

Gullev och Sjole lät resa (stenen) efter Ingefast,  Järunds son.
