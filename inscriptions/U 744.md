## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gyða lét reisa stein at "Þordjarf, bónda sinn, son "Guðlaugar, ok bjó í "Harvistôðum. "Balli risti stein.
```

## Translation to English

Gyða had the stone raised in memory of Þordjarfr, her husbandman, Guðlaug's son, and (he) lived in Harvistaðir. Balli carved the stone.

## Translation to Swedish

Gyda lät resa stenen efter Tordjärv, sin man, Gudlög son, och han bodde i Härvesta. Balle ristade stenen.
