## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Al/"Ali/"Alli let ræisa stæin þenna æftiR sik sialfan. Hann tok "Knuts giald a "Ænglandi. "Guð hialpi hans and.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ál/Áli/"Alli lét reisa stein þenna eptir sik sjalfan. Hann tók "Knúts gjald á "Englandi. "Guð hjalpi hans ônd.
```

## Translation to English

Ál/Áli/Alli had this stone raised in memory of himself. He took Knútr's payment in England. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Åle/Alle lät resa denna sten efter sig själv. Han tog Knuts gäld i England. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
