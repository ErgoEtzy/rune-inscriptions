## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sveinn ok "Ketilmundr ok "Erinmundr þeir létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Tíðkuma, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Sveinn and Ketilmundr and Erinmundr, they had this stone raised in memory of Tíðkumi, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Sven och Kättilmund och Ärenmund de lät resa denna sten efter Tidkume, sin fader.
