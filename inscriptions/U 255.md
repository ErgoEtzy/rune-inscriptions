## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Illugi lét reisa [stei]na [epti]r "Bjôrn, bróður sinn, ok "Gillaug at son sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Illugi had the stones raised in memory of Bjôrn, his brother; and Gillaug in memory of her son.

## Translation to Swedish

Illuge lät resa stenarna efter Björn, sin broder, och Gillög efter sin son.
