## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
§A "Geirfríðr(?) ok "Geirmarr létu hôggva §B at "Auðmund. "Grímr "Skald hjó.
```

## Translation to English

§A Geirfríðr(?) and Geirmarr had (this) cut §B in memory of Auðmundr. Grímr Skald cut.

## Translation to Swedish

Gerfrid(?) och Germar lät hugga efter Ödmund. Grim skald högg.
