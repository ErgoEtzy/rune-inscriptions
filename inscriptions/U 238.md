## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Astriðr let ræisa þenna stæin æftiR sun sinn "Svæin ok "Ulf, bonda sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ástríðr lét reisa þenna stein eptir son sinn "Svein ok "Ulf, bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ástríðr had this stone raised in memory of her son Sveinn and Ulfr her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Astrid lät resa denna sten efter sin son Sven och Ulv, sin make.
