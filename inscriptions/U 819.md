## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Svæinn/"Stæinn/"Sæinn let ræisa stæin þenna at faður ... <-þah> "SigriðaR boanda. "Guð hiælpi sal hans. "Balli risti.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sveinn/"Steinn/"Seinn lét reisa stein þenna at fôður ... <-þah> "Sigríðar bónda. "Guð hjalpi sál hans. "Balli risti.
```

## Translation to English

Sveinn/Steinn/Seinn had this stone raised in memory of (his) father ... Sigríðr's husbandman. May God help his soul. Balli carved.

## Translation to Swedish

Sven/Sten/Sen lät resa denna sten efter [sin] fader ... Sigrids man. Gud hjälpe hans själ. Balle ristade.
