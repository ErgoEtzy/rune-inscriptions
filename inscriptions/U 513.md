## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ônundr ok "Eiríkr ok "Hákon ok "Ingvarr reis[tu stein] þenna eptir "Ragnar, bróður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

Ônundr and Eiríkr and Hákon and Ingvarr raised this stone in memory of Ragnarr, their brother. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Anund och Erik och Håkon och Ingvar reste denna sten efter Ragnar, sin broder. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
