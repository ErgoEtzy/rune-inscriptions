## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Guðfastr ok "Þrondr letu retta stæin æftiR faður sinn "Hals, ok "Frøyguðr æftiR bonda sinn. "Þorðr hiogg runaR þessaR val.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Guðfastr ok "Þróndr létu rétta stein eptir fôður sinn "Hals, ok "Freygunnr eptir bónda sinn. "Þórðr hjó rúnar þessar vel.
```

## Translation to English

Guðfastr and Þróndr had the stone erected in memory of their father Hals; and Freygunnr in memory of her husbandman. Þórðr cut these runes well.

## Translation to Swedish

Gudfast och Trond lät uppresa stenen efter sin fader Hals, och Frögunn efter sin man. Tord högg dessa runor väl.
