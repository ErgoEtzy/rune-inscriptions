## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Halfdan ræisþi stæi[n þann]si at "Ragnvald, faður sinn, ok "Dan, broður sinn, þrottaR þiagna.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Halfdan reisti stei[n þenn]a at "Ragnvald, fôður sinn, ok "Dan, bróður sinn, þróttar þegna.
```

## Translation to English

Halfdan raised this stone in memory of Ragnvaldr, his father, and Danr, his brother, Þegns of strength.

## Translation to Swedish

Halvdan reste denna sten efter Ragnvald, sin fader, och Dan, sin broder, dugande män.

## Other information

Påträffades vid grävning för källare 1947. Salberger (1987b:146-151) läser þruta þiak(n)a.
