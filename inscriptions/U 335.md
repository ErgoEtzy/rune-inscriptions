## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Holmi lét reisa stein þenna ok brú þessa eptir "Hæru(?), fôður sinn, húskarl "Sigrøðar.
```

## Translation to English

Holmi had this stone raised and this bridge (made) in memory of Hæra, his father, Sigrøðr's housecarl.

## Translation to Swedish

Holme lät resa denna sten och (göra) denna bro efter Hära, sin fader, Sigröds huskarl.
