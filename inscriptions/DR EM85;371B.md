## Translation to English

§PA Let Oddulfr interpret! Let Oddulfr send Auðríkr/Eyríkr ... Auðríkr/Eyríkr ... Oddulfr ... §PB <auþikR> ... wretched sword <ul---li> ... §PC ... now wretched sword. §QA May Ulfr overcome the wound! Let the staff of oppression nevertheless effect §QC ... now (a) wretched sword.
