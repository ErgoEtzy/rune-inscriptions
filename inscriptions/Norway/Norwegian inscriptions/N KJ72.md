## Translation to English

§A I, Wiwaz, made the runes after Woduridaz, my lord. §QA For me, Woduridaz, three daughters, the most distinguished of the heirs, prepared the stone. §QB <...z> (= Wiwaz)(?) (erected) the stone for Woduridaz. Three daughters shared the inheritance, the closest family heirs.
