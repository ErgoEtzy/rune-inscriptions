## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Holmbiorn let ræisa stæin at sik sialfan. Hann vaR mildr mataR ok malsrisinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Holmbjôrn lét reisa stein at sik sjalfan. Hann var mildr matar ok málsrisinn.
```

## Translation to English

Holmbjôrn had the stone raised in memory of himself. He was generous with food and eloquent.

## Translation to Swedish

Holmbjörn lät resa stenen efter sig själv. Han var matmild och målsnäll.
