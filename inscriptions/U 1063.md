## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Stynfríðr/"Steinfríðr ok "Holmfríðr létu reisa stein eptir ... [f]ôður sinn ... "[Jô]rundr(?). "Œpir risti rúnar.
```

## Translation to English

Stynfríðr/Steinfríðr and Holmfríðr had the stone raised in memory of ... their father ... Jôrundr(?) Œpir carved the runes.

## Translation to Swedish

Stynfrid/Stenfrid och Holmfrid lät resa stenen efter ..., sin fader ... Jorund(?). Öpir ristade runorna.
