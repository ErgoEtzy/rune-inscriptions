## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Forkunnr ok "Gamall létu reisa stein þenna eptir "Una/"Unna, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi anda "Una/"Unna.
```

## Translation to English

Forkunnr and Gamall had this stone raised in memory of Uni/Unni, their father. May God help Uni's/Unni's spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Forkunn och Gammal lät resa denna sten efter Une/Unne, sin fader. Gud hjälpe Unes/Unnes ande.
