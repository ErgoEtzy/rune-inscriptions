## Translation to English

§A Gunnhildr had this stone raised in memory of Auðbjôrn, her husbandman. May Christ help Auðbjôrn's soul into light and paradise. §B May Christ and Saint Michael help the souls of Auðbjôrn and Gunnhildr into light and paradise.
