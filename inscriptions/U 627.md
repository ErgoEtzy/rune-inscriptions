## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ônundr, "Gautr ok "Njósi(?) reistu stein þenna eptir "Ásbjôrn, fôður sinn. "Guð dróttinn hjalpi ôndu hans.
```

## Translation to English

Ônundr, Gautr and Njósi(?) raised this stone in memory of Ásbjôrn, their father. May Lord God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Anund, Göt och Nyse reste denna sten efter Åsbjörn, sin fader. Herren Gud hjälpe hans ande.

## Other information
