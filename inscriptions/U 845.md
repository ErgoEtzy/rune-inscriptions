## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gísl ok "Ásgeirr reistu stein þenna eptir "Bjôrn, bróður sinn, annan eptir fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Gísl and Ásgeirr raised this stone in memory of Bjôrn, their brother, the other in memory of their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Gisl och Åsger reste denna sten efter Björn, sin broder, den andra efter sin fader.
