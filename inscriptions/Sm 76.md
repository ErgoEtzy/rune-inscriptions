## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian


## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Tófa reisti stein þenna eptir "Vráa, fôður sinn, stallara "Hákonar jarls.
```

## Translation to English

Tófa raised this stone in memory of Vrái, his father, Earl Hákon's marshal.

## Translation to Swedish

Tova reste denna sten efter Vråe, sin fader, Håkon jarls stallare.
