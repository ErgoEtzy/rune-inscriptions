## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ólôf reisti stein þenna eptir "Hofi/"Høfi/"Hyfi, bónda sinn góðan.
```

## Translation to English

Ólôf raised this stone in memory of Hofir/Høfir/Hyfir, her good husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Olov reste denna sten efter Hove/Höve/Hyve, sin gode make.
