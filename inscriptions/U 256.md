## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Ingialdr ok "HæmingR letu ræisa stæina æftiR "Ulf, faður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ingjaldr ok "Hemingr létu reisa steina eptir "Ulf, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ingjaldr and Hemingr had the stones raised in memory of Ulfr, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Ingjald och Häming lät resa stenarna efter Ulv, sin fader.
