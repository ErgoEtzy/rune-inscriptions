## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Gyða reisti stein þenna eptir "Þorbjôrn, bónda sinn, mjôk góðan þegn. En "Þórðr reist rúnar þessar.
```

## Translation to English

Gyða raised this stone in memory of Þorbjôrn, her husbandman, a very good þegn. And Þórðr carved these runes.
