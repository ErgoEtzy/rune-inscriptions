## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Fastbjôrn ok "Jarl létu rétta stein at "Þorstein, fôður sinn, "Hringja.
```

## Translation to English

Fastbjôrn and Jarl had the stone erected in memory of Þorsteinn, their father. Hringja.

## Translation to Swedish

Fastbjörn och Jarl lät uppresa stenen efter Torsten, sin fader, Ringa (efter sin man?).
