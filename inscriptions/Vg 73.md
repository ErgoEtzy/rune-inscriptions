## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Karr ok "Kali/"Kalli ræistu stæin þannsi æftiR "Veurð, faður sinn, miok goðan þegn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Kárr ok "Kali/"Kalli reistu stein þenna eptir "Véurð, fôður sinn, mjôk góðan þegn.
```

## Translation to English

Kárr and Kali/Kalli raised this stone in memory of Véurðr, their father, a very good Þegn.

## Translation to Swedish

Kar och Kale reste denna sten efter Veurd, sin fader, en mycket god tägn.
