## Translation to English

§P Tobbi(?)/Tubbi(?) and Gunn-... ... in memory of Sibbi(?), his good son. Œpir carved the runes. §Q Tobbi(?)/Tubbi(?) and Gunn-... ... the stone in memory of Sibbi, his good son. Œpir carved the runes.
