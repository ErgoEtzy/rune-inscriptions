## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ketilbjôrn ok "Þorbjôrn ok "Þorketill ok "Farmaðr ok "Sveinn reistu stein at "Ígulbjôrn, fôður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Ketilbjôrn and Þorbjôrn and Þorketill and Farmaðr and Sveinn raised the stone in memory of Ígulbjôrn, their father.

## Translation to Swedish

Kättilbjörn och Torbjörn och Torkel och Farman och Sven reste stenen efter Igulbjörn, sin fader.
