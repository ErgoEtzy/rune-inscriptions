## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"HolmlaugR ok "Holmfriðr lata ræisa stæina æftiR "Fasta ok "Sigfast, sunu sina. ÞæiR dou i hvitavaðum.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Holmlaugr ok "Holmfríðr láta reisa steina eptir "Fasta ok "Sigfast, sonu sína. Þeir dóu í hvítaváðum.
```

## Translation to English

Holmlaugr and Holmfríðr have raised the stones in memory of Fasti and Sigfastr, their sons. They died in (their) christening robes.

## Translation to Swedish

Holmlög och Holmfrid låter resa stenarna efter Faste och Sigfast, sina söner. De dog i vita kläder.
