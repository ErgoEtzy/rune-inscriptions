## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"ÞoriR let haggva stæin at "Binu(?), faður sinn, "Kætilvi at bonda sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Þórir lét hôggva stein at "Bínu(?), fôður sinn, "Ketilvé at bónda sinn.
```

## Translation to English

Þórir had the stone cut in memory of Bína(?), his father; Ketilvé in memory of her husbandman.

## Translation to Swedish

Tore lät hugga stenen efter Bina(?) sin fader, Kättilvi efter sin man.
