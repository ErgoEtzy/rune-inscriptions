## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ásgautr reisti stein þenna eptir "Svein, fôður sinn. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans ok "Guðs móðir.
```

## Translation to English

Ásgautr raised the stone in memory of Sveinn, his father. May God and God's mother help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

Åsgöt reste sten denna efter Sven, sin fader. Gud hjälpe hans ande, och Guds moder.
