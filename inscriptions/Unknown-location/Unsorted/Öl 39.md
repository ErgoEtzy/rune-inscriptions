## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"Svæinn gærði æftiR sinn faður, "Vikar, sunn æinigi sialfR ræisti stæin.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Sveinn gerði eptir sinn fôður, "Víkar, sonr einigi sjalfr reisti stein.
```

## Translation to English

Sveinn made in memory of his father, Víkarr, the only son raised the stone himself.

## Translation to Swedish

Sven gjorde efter sin fader Vikar, ende sonen reste själv stenen.
