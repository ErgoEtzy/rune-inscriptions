## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
... ok "Drængi ok "Sigstæinn, þæiR brøðr ræistu stæi[n æftiR] "Vifast. "Guð hialpi and hans.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
... ok "Drengi ok "Sigsteinn, þeir brœðr reistu stei[n eptir] "Véfast. "Guð hjalpi ônd hans.
```

## Translation to English

... and Drengi and Sigsteinn, these brothers raised the stone (in memory of) Véfastr. May God help his spirit.

## Translation to Swedish

... och Dränge och Sigsten, dessa bröder reste stenen [efter] Vifast. Gud hjälpe hans ande.
