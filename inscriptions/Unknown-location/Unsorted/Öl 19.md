## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"Ástríðr lét reisa stein þenna eptir "Bjôrn, bónda sinn. "Guð hjalpi hans sál.
```

## Translation to English

Ástríðr had this stone raised in memory of Bjôrn, her husband. May God help his soul.

## Translation to Swedish

Estrid lät resa denna sten efter Björn, sin man. Gud hjälpe hans själ.
