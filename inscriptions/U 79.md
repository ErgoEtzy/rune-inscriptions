```text
§A "Gýi ok "Sveinn þeir reistu stein at "Bersu/"Birsu, fôður sinn góðan. §B "Ernfastr hjó stein at "Bersu/"Birsu, bónda "Végerðar, son "Þorgerðar góðan.
```

## Translation to English

§A Gýi and Sveinn, they raised the stone in memory of Bersa/Birsa, their good father. §B Ernfastr cut the stone in memory of Bersa/Birsa, Végerðr's husbandman, the good son of Þorgerðr.

## Translation to Swedish

Gy och Sven de reste stenen efter Bärsa, sin gode fader. Ärnfast högg stenen efter Bärsa, Vigärds make, Torgärds gode son.
