## Translation to English

§P Guðnjótr Þóru(?)'s son had this stone erected and made the bridge in memory of his brothers Ásbjôrn and in memory of Guðlafr. §Q Guðnjótr Þrór's son had this stone erected and made the bridge in memory of his brothers Ásbjôrn and in memory of Guðlafr.

## Translation to Swedish

Gudnjut, Toras(?)/Tros son, lät uppresa denna sten och gjorde bron efter sina bröder, Åsbjörn och efter Gudlev.
