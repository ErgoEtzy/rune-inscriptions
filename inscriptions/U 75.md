## Normalization to Old West Norse


## Translation to English

Sigviðr had this stone raised in memory of his father Egviðr/Hegviðr and his mother Holmfríðr, and Jôfurfríðr (also raised)?

## Translation to Swedish

Sigvid lät resa denna sten efter sin fader Egvid/Hägvid och sin moder Holmfrid, och Jovurfrid.
