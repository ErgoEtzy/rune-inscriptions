## Transliteration

```text
...ikaiR lit resa sten auk ' kera ' buro ' eftiR ' siiterf · buroþur · sin
```

## Normalisation to Old Scandinavian

```text
"...gæiRR let ræisa stæin ok gæra bro æftiR "Sædiarf/"Sigdiarf, broður sinn.
```

## Normalization to Old West Norse

```text
"...geirr lét reisa stein ok gera brú eptir "Sædjarf/"Sigdjarf, bróður sinn.
```

## Translation to English

...-geirr had the stone raised and the bridge made in memory of Sædjarfr/Sigdjarfr, his brother.

## Translation to Swedish

…-ger lät resa stenen och göra bron efter Sädjärv/Sigdjärv, sin broder.
